■ 이벤트 기간

- 이벤트 진행 기간: 2025년 7월 17일(목) 점검 후 ~ 8월 20일(수) 오후 11시 59분 59초까지

- 보상 수령 기간: 2025년 7월 17일(목) 점검 후 ~ 8월 27일(수) 오후 11시 59분까지

■ 이벤트 참여 방법

- 이벤트 리스트에서 ‘골드리치의 비밀금고’ 이벤트를 선택 후 ‘참여하기’ 버튼을 클릭하여 이벤트에 참여할 수 있습니다.

- 이벤트 시작 완료 후, 레벨 범위 몬스터를 처치하면 ‘금고 티켓 1장 교환권’이 드롭됩니다.

- '금고 티켓 1장 교환권’은 메이플ID당 하루 50장까지 획득할 수 있습니다.

※ 드롭되는 금고 티켓 1장 교환권은 1회 거래가 가능하며, 이후에는 월드 내 나의 캐릭터 간 이동만 가능합니다.

※ ‘금고 티켓 1장 교환권’은 2025년 7월 17일(목) 점검 후 ~ 2025년 8월 20일(수) 오후 11시 59분까지 획득할 수 있습니다.

- ‘금고 티켓 1장 교환권’ 사용 시 금고에 응모할 수 있는 금고 티켓을 1개 획득할 수 있습니다.

- ‘황금 금고’ 또는 ‘다이아 금고’를 통해 진행중인 라운드의 경품에 응모할 수 있습니다.

■ 라운드 진행 기간

- 황금 금고: 매일 오전 0시 0분 0초 ~ 오후 11시 59분 59초까지

- 다이아 금고: 매주 목요일 오전 0시 0분 0초 ~ 다음주 수요일 오후 11시 59분 59초까지

■ 응모 방법

- 금고 티켓 10개를 소모하여 현재 진행중인 라운드의 금고 비밀번호를 예측할 수 있습니다.

- 비밀번호는 1부터 9999999까지 입력 가능하며, 한 라운드에 동일한 비밀번호는 여러 번 입력할 수 없습니다.

- 한 라운드마다 각 금고에 최대 15번까지 응모할 수 있습니다.

※ 황금 금고에 15번, 다이아 금고에 15번씩 응모 가능합니다.

※ 한 라운드에는 하나의 넥슨ID로만 응모 가능합니다.

※ 한 라운드에는 같은 넥슨ID에 속한 메이플ID 중 1개로만 응모할 수 있습니다.

※ 한 라운드가 진행되는 동안에는 처음 응모한 월드에서만 이어서 응모할 수 있습니다.

- 이벤트 리스트에서 ‘골드리치의 비밀금고’ 이벤트의 도움말 메뉴를 통해 응모 기록을 확인할 수 있습니다.

※ 응모한 월드에서만 응모 정보를 조회할 수 있습니다.

■ 황금 금고 참여 보상

- 황금 금고의 각 라운드에 1회 응모하면 참여 보상을 획득할 수 있습니다.

- 참여 보상은 이벤트 기간이 종료되기 전까지 수령할 수 있습니다.

      • If you fail an exploration (such as by not surfacing before you run out of oxygen), you can retry up to two times.

    • Use the right ladder on the boat to dive into the Blue Hole! Catch as many fish as you can and make it back to the surface before you run out of air or before 60 minutes elapse!
      • When there is 1 minute remaining in the time limit, you will receive a notice reminding you to finish your dive beore time expires.

    Blue Hole Exploration Controls and Features:

    Function Controls and Details
    Basic Movement
    • W: Move Up
    • A: Move Left
    • S: Move Down
    • D: Move Right
    Swim Faster
    • Use W/A/S/D to move while holding Shift.
    • Swimming faster consumes additional O2.
    Melee / Use Knife
    • Left Mouse Button (LMB)
    • All fish can be damaged by melee attacks.
    Fire Weapon
    • Hold Right Mouse Button (RMB) to aim.
    • Press LMB to fire.
    • While holding RMB to aim, game speed will slow to help you aim.
    • Works with the equipped Harpoon, Underwater Rifle, and Red Sniper weapons.
    Switch Weapon
    • Press Tab to switch between Harpoon, Underwater Rifle, and Red Sniper weapons.
    Use Exploration Equipment
    • Press C to use the equipped Exploration Equipment item.
    Switch Exploration Equipment
    • Press R to switch between available Exploration Equipment.
    Open Diving Inventory
    • Press Tilde (~) to open up the Fish PDA and review the fish you've hunted and the meat you've obtained.
    Open Fish PDA
    • Press Esc to open up your inventory, allowing you to view your equipment and acquired fish.
    • The dive is paused while this menu is open, however time in the menu will still count against the 1-hour time limit.
    Use/Interact
    • Press Space to interact with equipment and objects found in the Blue Hole.
    • Use while at the surface to return to the boat.

    • O2: Shows your remaining oxygen. Run out, and your dive will end with none of the fish you've caught coming back with you!
      • Oxygen is slowly consumed while diving, even when completely stationary.
      • When swimming faster with the Shift button, O2 will be consumed at a faster rate.
      • Being hit by fish marked with a red dot will also deplete your O2.
    • Depth: Shows how far you've dived. Exceeding the maximum depth of your Diving Suit will cause O2 to deplete faster.
    • KG: Shows how much weight you're carrying, as well as the maximum amount you can carry.
      • Carrying over the maximum will greatly reduce your movement speed, and eventually you will be prevented from picking up more fish.
      • A Weight icon will appear over your head when you're overencumbered.
      • The more weight you're carrying, the more time it will take to use the Escape Pod to surface from the depths.

    • Pressing Esc will bring up the Diving Inventory, showing your current equipment and acquired fish.
    • Fish that you do not wish to carry around can be discarded by selecting the undesired fish and pressing Space. Multiple fish can be selected and discarded at once with Left Ctrl.
    • If you wish to immediately give up on the current dive, press Tilde (~) from this menu. This will count as a loss, and any fish you've obtained will be lost.

    • The Escape Pod allows you to immediately return to the boat without needing to swim back to the surface.
      • Use it by swimming near the Escape Pod and pressing Space.

    • Oxygen Tanks refill your O2 back to full when picked up.
      • Use it by swimming near the Oxygen Tank and pressing Space.

    • Weapon Boxes provide one weapon selected at random when picked up:
      • Kenta's Harpoon: Replaces the equipped melee weapon for the remainder of the dive.
      • Frozen Tuna: Replaces the equipped melee weapon for the remainder of the dive.
      • Red Sniper Rifle: Can be used instead of your Underwater Rifle for the remainder of the dive. Can be ugpraded in the Smartphone's Weapon Shop menu.
      • Use it by swimming near the Weapon Box and pressing Space.

    • Ammo Boxes refill your Underwater Rifle and Death Sniper ammo when picked up.
      • Use it by swimming near the Ammo Box and pressing Space.

    • Equipment Boxes reward one of the following items, selected at random:
      • Oxygen Capsule: Restores 50% of maximum O2 when used.
      • Rifle Upgrade Kit: Increases rifle damage by 10% for the rest of the dive when used.
      • Underwater Scooter: Increases movement speed for a set period of time.
      • Emergency Pager: Allows you to end the dive and immediately resurface without losing any fish.
      • Use it by swimming near the Equipment Box and pressing Space.
      • Equipment can be used with the R key, and you can cycle through your Equipment with the C key.

    • When you defeat a large-sized fish, swim near it and hold Space to harvest the meat and put it in your cargo box.
      • Larger fish may require multiple harvests to get all of the meat, so keep at it until the fish disappears!

    • Some fish when harpooned will fight against you, and you'll need to reel them in!
      • Repeatedly press the Space bar as quickly as possible to pull the fish in.
      • If successful, the fish will be hauled in and placed in the Cargo Box.
      • If failed, the fish will become unhooked and swim away.

    Bancho Sushi

    Take the fish you've caught to Bancho Sushi to feed the clientele of Gold Beach!

    Before opening the restaurant, you'll want to update the Menu and manage the employees to make the most out of the day's dining experience.

    Updating the Menu

    • Press 1 to open up the Menu UI, where you can determine what your customers will be able to order that day.

    • Click on "+ Add Menu" to select a new dish to add to the Menu.
      • Additional Menu slots are unlocked through story progression, up to a maximum of 7 Menu slots.
      • The basic Menu items of Norimaki and Green Tea are not counted towards your current Menu slots.

    • You can offer as much of each dish as you have the ingredients for each recipe. Press left or right to adjust the recipe's quantity.

    • Once set, you can check to see the dish listed on the Menu.

    • In the Menu UI, you can select a chosen dish and press Space to show options, including adding more ingredients, enhancing the dish, or removing it from the Menu.

    • Dishes can be enhanced up to Lv. 5 if you have the fish to meet the requirements.
    • Norimaki and Green Tea cannot be enhanced and will remain at Lv. 1.
    • The higher the dish level, the more Gold it will sell for.
    • Enhancements are permanent, and will remain for future restaurant days!

    Hosting Parties

    • On certain in-story days, Bancho Sushi will have a Hosting Party, where specific types of sushi will sell for considerably more than normal.
      • Day 15: Jellyfish Party
        • Jellyfish dishes sell for 5x the normal price.
        • Applies to sushi made from Fried Egg Jellyfish, White Spotted Jellyfish, and Comb Jelly.
      • Day 20: Tuna Party
        • Tuna dishes sell for 2.5x the normal price.
        • Applies to sushi made from Yellowfin Tuna and Bluefin Tuna.
      • Day 25: Shark Party
        • Shark dishes sell for 1.5x the normal price.
        • Applies to sushi made from Whitetip Reefshark, Smooth Hammerhead, Copper Shark, Thresher Shark, Longnose Sawshark, Zebra Shark, Cookiecutter Shark, Frilled Shark, Rhinochimaeridae, and Megamouth Shark.
      • In-Story Days only progress with a successful dive and shift at Bancho Sushi.
      • Parties repeat every 15 in-story days (ie: Day 30 will be a Jellyfish Party, Day 35 will be a Tuna Party, Day 40 will be a Shark party...).
    • A notice in the top-right corner of the screen on the boat and in Bancho Sushi's prep screen will note when a party is within three days to give you time to prepare!

    Managing the Employees

    • You can't do everything alone, and the more customers that come, the more help you'll need!
    • Employees can be hired as you complete certain Chapter Missions:
      • 1st Hall Staff: Clear Chapter 3.
      • 1st Kitchen Staff: Clear Chapter 4.
      • 2nd Hall Staff: Clear Chapter 7.
      • 2nd Kitchen Staff: Clear Chapter 10.
    • A maximum of 2 Hall Staff and 2 Kitchen Staff can be active at a time, and hired staff that are inactive will be placed in the Waiting Room.
    • Kitchen Staff improve the speed at which dishes are prepared.
    • Hall Staff assist you in serving the customers.
    • Some Staff have special skills that can improve their performance!

    • When Bancho Sushi is closed, press 2 to go to the staff menu and press Left Ctrl to see the list of employees to hire.
    • Employees will cost Gold to hire, with more talented employees (rightfully!) demanding higher pay for their service.
    • Select an employee and use the "Details" option to see their full information before hiring them.

    • Once hired, you can add Employees to an open slot in the main restaurant window.
    • Select the slot you wish them to fill and add them from the Waiting Room.

    Customer Service

    • Once the restaurant is opened, customers will enter the shop and order a dish after a short time.
    • As more Chapter Missions are completed, more customers will arrive to the shop each day.

    Green Tea

    • Some customers will order Green Tea first, which will earn more Gold when served.
    • When you see a customer with the Green Tea speech bubble, move close to them and press Space to begin serving tea.
    • If the customer is not served promptly, the speech bubble will start to fill with red. If the speech bubble fills fully, then they will stop requesting tea and begin picking a dish instead.

    • Hold S to pour the tea, and try to get as close to the green fill marker on the right side of the cup.
    • If the tea overflows or too little is poured, then you will receive a Bad grade, and earn no Gold.
    • Pouring it close to the line will result in a Good or Perfect rating, earning bonus Gold.

    Grate Wasabi

    • Every dish costs some Wasabi to create, and it will deplete over time. If the Wasabi does run out, dish preparation will pause until it is refilled.
    • To replenish the Wasabi, go to the Wasabi Meter on the far right of the restaurant and press Space to begin to grate.

    • Repeatedly press W to quickly grate fresh Wasabi and refill the meter.
    • The minigame will automatically close when the meter reaches 100%, and you can manually close it with ESC or C.

    Serving Dishes

    • When a customer decides on a dish, it will appear in their word bubble, and the kitchen will automatically start to work on it.
    • As the customer waits for their dish, the word bubble will slowly start to fill with red as they become more and more impatient.
    • Once the word bubble fills with red, the customer will be angry at your slow service and leave the shop, taking their money with them!

    • When the progress bar is filled, the dish is complete and can be served to the customer.
    • Move over to Bancho and press Space to pick up the dish.
    • Serving the customer their dish will reward you with Gold!

    • While holding a dish, your speech bubble will show the dish that you're serving.
    • Take the dish to a customer with the same dish in their speech bubble and press Space to serve it.
    • You can hold only one dish at a time, and you can press Q to discard the dish (such as if the customer that wanted the dish has left).

    Cleaning Up

    • After finishing their meal, some quite inconsiderate customers will leave their place dirty.
    • Move to their seat and press Space to begin cleaning.
    • Repeatedly press A and D to clean up the seat.

    • Cleaning a dirty seat will reward a small amount of Gold.
    • New customers will prefer clean seats over dirty ones.
    • If a dirty seat is not cleaned by the time a customer seated there is ready to order, they will instead leave the restaurant!
    • Additionally, customers at dirty seats will not order Green Tea.

    Inven公告


    메이플스토리, 6차 전직 신규 '어센트 스킬' 포함 여름 2차 업데이트 적용


    넥슨이 17일, PC MMORPG '메이플스토리'의 여름 2차 업데이트를 실시했다. 이번 패치에서는 6차 전직 신규 스킬 코어 '어센트 스킬'이 신규 직업 '렌'을 포함, 47개 전 직업에 도입됐다. 해당 스킬은 보스 전투 상황에서 최대 3회까지 재사용 대기시간 없이 발동 가능하며, 스킬 시전 시 일시적으로 무적 상태가 되어 강력한 공격을 사용하게 된다.



    여름 이벤트 '황혼빛 전야제'도 2차 이벤트에 돌입했다. 8월 20일까지 레벨 범위 내 몬스터 1,000마리를 처치하면 이벤트가 시작되고, 이후 300마리마다 등급을 올릴 수 있다. 등급이 올라갈수록 성장 아이템 보상이 늘어나며, 최대 9단계까지 도전 가능하다. 등급 상승에 도전할지, 현재 보상을 받을지 직접 선택할 수 있다.

    이외 출석 이벤트 '에버니아의 선물'에 맞춘 '스페셜 답사'에서는 출석 구간마다 하루 두 가지 보상이 제공되며, 몬스터 처치 게이지를 채우면 추가 경험치와 함께 최대 30회까지 보상을 받을 수 있는 '즐거운 오아시스' 이벤트도 동시 진행된다.

    '골드리치의 비밀 금고' 이벤트도 열린다. 참여자는 8월 20일까지 몬스터 처치로 금고 티켓을 모아, 10장씩 사용해 비밀번호를 입력해 응모할 수 있다. 매 라운드마다 한 번만 응모된 숫자 중 가장 작은 번호가 당첨 번호로 선정된다. 황금 금고는 매일 한 라운드씩 35회, 다이아 금고는 주 1회씩 5회 열린다. '솔 에르다 조각', '영겁의 황금 태엽', '리미티드 라벨', 데미지 스킨, 메이플 홈 테마 등 다양한 아이템이 제공된다.

    업데이트와 함께 '넥슨온' 앱의 '넥슨링크'를 통한 원격 플레이 기능이 추가돼, PC에서 실행 중인 게임을 모바일로 연동해 플레이할 수 있게 됐다. 보스 몬스터 '가디언 엔젤 슬라임'과 '발드릭스'의 패턴 개선, 장비/상점/캐시샵 UI 전면 개편도 포함됐다. 또한 8월 21일까지 PC방 일일/누적 접속 보상이 지급되며, 8월 20일까지 특별 아이템이 포함된 PC방 전용 프리미엄 기프트샵도 운영된다.

    각종 이벤트와 새 스킬, 시스템 개편 등 구체적인 업데이트 내역은 '메이플스토리' 공식 홈페이지에서 확인할 수 있다.
