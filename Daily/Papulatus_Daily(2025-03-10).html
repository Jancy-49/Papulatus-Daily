Tanjiro's Maple Adventure


Event Period: 12th March 2025, After v240 ~ 22nd April 2025, 2359hrs

Available to Level 101 or above, Zero character who has completed Story Quest Chapter 2.

Developer Notes

Heroes of MapleStory, Total Concentration! The Demon Slayer Corps have arrived in Maple World.
Fill the adventure journal as the Tanjiro class from the Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba animation, experience the Demon Slayer Corps' training alongside other characters, and receive luxurious rewards!
Arigato Gozaimasu!

A [Tanjiro] class character is required in the same World to participate, but [Tanjiro] class characters cannot participate in this event.

To start, look for [Demon Slayer] First Encounter with the Demon Slayer Corps quest in the event list at the left side of the screen.

⇣

Look for [Demon Slayer] Tanjiro's Maple Adventure quest in the event list to unlock passive skill - Tanjiro's Support and active skill - Tanjiro's Support: Dancing Flash.
Important! A [Tanjiro] class character is required in the same World in order to accept the quest.



Tanjiro's Support can be found in the Beginner category under Skill and is available until 22nd April 2025, 2359hrs.

⇣

The skill effects provided by Tanjiro's Support change based on your [Tanjiro] class character's level and the skill effect unlock date.

    • Zenitsu and Inosuke's Maple Adventure


    Event Period: 12th March 2025, After v240 ~ 22nd April 2025, 2359hrs

    Available to Level 101 or above, Zero character who has completed Story Quest Chapter 2.
    [Tanjiro] class characters cannot participate in this event.

    To start, look for [Demon Slayer] First Encounter with the Demon Slayer Corps quest in the event list at the left side of the screen.

    ⇣



    Talk to NPC Inosuke Hashibira located at Nameless Mansion and look for [Demon Slayer] Zenitsu and Inosuke's Maple Adventure quest to unlock Zenitsu and Inosuke passive skill.

    ⇣



    Zenitsu and Inosuke can be found in the Beginner category under Skill and is available until 22nd April 2025, 2359hrs.

    ⇣



    When entering a map with monsters within your character's level range, Zenitsu's gauge is automatically enabled. Defeat monsters within your character's level range to charge the gauge.
    For every 100 monsters defeated, Inosuke is summoned to attack nearby enemies with Beast Breathing Fifth Fang: Crazy Cutting.

    ⇣



    Once Zenitsu's gauge is fully charged, Thunder Breathing First Form: Thunderclap and Flash Sixfold is automatically activated to attack nearby enemies.



    When Thunder Breathing First Form: Thunderclap and Flash Sixfold is activated, a large amount of EXP can be obtained by the character.
    Zenitsu and Inosuke can be used up to 20 times per day per character and the limits reset daily at 0000hrs.

    Note:
  • Monsters within the character's level range have a level difference of +20 or -20 compared to your character.
  • Beast Breathing Fifth Fang: Crazy Cutting and Thunder Breathing First Form: Thunderclap and Flash Sixfold will only activate on maps containing enemies within the character's level range. It will not activate on maps affected by an Elite Boss's Level 4 curse, event maps, or other special maps.
  • If another player has already activated Beast Breathing Fifth Fang: Crazy Cutting on the same map, the next instance will activate only after the previous one has ended.
  • If another player has already activated Thunder Breathing First Form: Thunderclap and Flash Sixfold on the same map, the next instance will activate only after the previous one has ended.
  • On instanced maps, Beast Breathing Fifth Fang: Crazy Cutting will activate immediately, regardless of other players.
  • On instanced maps, Thunder Breathing First Form: Thunderclap and Flash Sixfold will activate immediately, regardless of other players.
  • Moving to another map, changing channels or entering the Cash Shop will end Beast Breathing Fifth Fang: Crazy Cutting immediately.
  • Moving to another map, changing channels or entering the Cash Shop will end Thunder Breathing First Form: Thunderclap and Flash Sixfold immediately.
  • Beast Breathing Fifth Fang: Crazy Cutting and Thunder Breathing First Form: Thunderclap and Flash Sixfold are not visible to other players.

    When the game starts, the Gourd Mark will move left and right. When it reaches the yellow highlighted zone, press the 'NPC/Gather' key to break the gourd!

    21 ~ 40 10

  • The Demon Slayer Corps Training Experience can be repeated, and obtaining a total of 300 points will unlock a daily reward.
  • The Demon Slayer Corps Training Experience can still be played after obtaining 300 points, but excess points cannot be earned until midnight.
  • Accumulated points will reset everyday 0000hrs.

    Stages

    Stage Info
  • 1 Gameplay map = 1 Stage.
  • 1 Chapter = 20 Stages.
  • Challenge mode = 40 Stages.
  • In each chapter, random stages may appear when certain conditions are met.
  • Once a Stage is cleared, move to the next Stage by using the portal on the right side of the map.
  • If your HP reaches 0 in a Stage or the time has ran out, you will automatically exit and move back to the event map.

    Types of Stages
  • Normal Stage
      ↪Normal stage that takes up the majority of every chapter.
      ↪Contains complicated terrain and traps.
      ↪Monsters will constantly spawn in multiple phases. (e.g. once all phase 1 monsters are killed, phase 2 monsters will start to spawn)
      ↪Killing all monsters in all phases will clear the stage, and allow you to claim the reward and move to the next stage.
      ↪Time limit per stage: 3~5 minutes.

  • Elite Stage
      ↪Small map with simple terrain, Mid Boss stage where Elite monsters will appear.
      ↪Elite Monsters have high health and use complicated attack patterns.
      ↪Stage clear reward will always include "Eternal Stone".
      ↪Killing all monsters will clear the stage.
      ↪Time limit per stage: 3~5 minutes.

  • Boss Stage
      ↪Last stage of each Chapter where a Boss will appear.
      ↪Will appear on Stage 20 of Normal mode and Hardcore mode
      ↪Will appear every 8 stages on Challenge Mode
      ↪Boss Monsters have high health and use multiple attack patterns.
      ↪No separate reward on clear (reward given together with entire Chapter reward exclusive stage)
      ↪Killing all monsters will clear the stage.

  • Survival Stage
      ↪If the next map would be a Normal Stage, there is a chance that a Survival stage will appear instead of a Normal stage.
      ↪In a small map about 1 screen wide, large amounts of monsters will continuously spawn, and you only clear the stage when you survive for a set period of time.
      ↪On entering a Survival Stage, a warning message and player popup dialogue will appear.
      ↪When the timer runs out, all monsters in the map will vanish and you will be able to move onto the next Stage.

  • Destroy the target Stage
      ↪Uses the same map as Normal stages, and a warning message and player popup dialogue will appear.
      ↪Clear condition is changed from killing all monsters to killing the elite monster while protecting the altar.
      ↪Large amount of monsters will continuously spawn.
      ↪1 Elite Monster will be spawned at the Altar, but will have slightly lowered health and attack power based on the difficulty level.

    [Pharaoh's Treasure] Artifacts



    Artifacts
    Obtain artifacts when you clear stages.
    Total of 6 equip slots for artifacts and 15 storage slots for artifacts provided in your inventory.
    Can be obtained from Monster Drops or Artifact Altar
    New artifacts can be crafted by combining 2 you already have.


    Artifact types
  • Passive Stats Artifact : Basic artifact which increases various stats of your character.
  • Special Effect Artifact : Artifact with various special effects.

    Artifact rarity
  • Low class Artifacts
      ↪ Shows up in monster drops, artifact altar, most places where artifacts can be obtained.
  • Middle class Artifacts
      ↪ Obtain using artifact combinations.
      ↪ Can get from Stage clear (Artifact Altar) or shop at a low probability.
  • High class Artifacts
      ↪ Can only be obtained by crafting artifacts.

    Artifact Crafting


  • While the artifact crafting UI is open, drag and drop or double click Artifacts in the inventory to place them in the artifact crafting UI, and click combine button to attempt crafting


  • After completing Artifact crafting, placing another Artifact in the crafting UI will reset the crafting results, and you can try a new combination without closing the UI


  • When 2 artifacts in your possession are combined, a random artifact will appear.
  • Special combinations can be used to guarantee a certain artifact.

    Special Combination (fixed combination) Recipe


  • Once you succeed in crafting using a fixed recipe, it will be stored in the recipe book.
      ↪ recorded in 3 phases
      ↪ 1st success: 1 ingredient is revealed.
      ↪ 2nd success: 2 ingredients revealed.
      ↪ 3rd success: 2 ingredients and the results are revealed.
  • You can check currently revealed recipes by pressing the ? button in the crafting UI.
