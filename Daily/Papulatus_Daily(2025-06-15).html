Unscheduled Server Maintenance - June 14, 2025


[Updated 5:01 PM PDT] The maintenance has been completed and we have extended Cash Shop items in your inventory for 4 hours. Please restart your Nexon Launcher or Steam to make sure that you get the latest client! Thank you for your patience.

Hi Maplers,

We will be performing an unscheduled server maintenance starting on Saturday Afternoon PDT, June 14, 2025 to address the Sia Astelle Erda Link issues.

Maintenance will be starting on Saturday Afternoon PST, June 14, 2025 at 1:00 PM PDT (4:00 PM EDT / 10:00 PM CEST / 6:00 AM AEST June 15) . We anticipate the maintenance to last approximately 4 hours, concluding around 5:00 PM PDT (8:00 PM EDT / 2:00 AM CEST June 15 / 10:00 AM AEST June 15).

Times:

Saturday, June 14, 2025
PDT (UTC -7): 1:00 PM - 5:00 PM
EDT (UTC -4): 4:00 PM - 8:00 PM
CEST (UTC +2): 10:00 PM - 2:00 AM June 15
AEST (UTC +10): 6:00 AM June 15 - 10:00 AM June 15

What will be unavailable:

  • All MapleStory game servers.

Changes and Updates:

  • Sia Astelle
    • Resolved an issue where the Erda Link nodes of Sia Astelle were intermittently being reset.
      • For characters who lost their Origin skill, the skill has been restored to Level 1.
      • We’re continuing to investigate into each case of missing skill levels or node progression and will provide compensation based on each player’s specific situation. We apologize for the inconvenience and are working to get compensation out as soon as we can.
  • Sacred Symbol and Banned Users
    • We have identified and fixed an exploit involving content repetition of Sacred Symbols. Accounts have been temporarily banned for 7 days, and any Sacred Symbols intentionally claimed multiple times are to be removed.
  • Miracle Time Extension
    • The Miracle Time event for Hat Equipment will be extended until June 15, 2025 at 6:59 AM UTC.
  • [Updated 6/18/25] Hyperion World Perks:
    • Due to the recent maintenances and downtime, the Hyperion Perks for players in Hyperion will now run from June 18 (after maintenance) - June 21 at 11:59 PM UTC.
  • [Updated] Compensation:
    • Compensation for today's maintenance will be provided and distributed during the June 18, 2025 maintenance.
