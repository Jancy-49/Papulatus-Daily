    23rd April 2025 (After Maintenance) ~ 3rd June 2025, 2359hrs

    Developer Notes

    To celebrate the Victoria Cup, Henesys and Kerning City will face off in a soccer derby!
    For fast-paced and dynamic matches, soccer in the Victoria Cup will be played in 5v5 matches lasting 3 minutes.
    Powerful special skills have been prepared for you to turn the game in your favour.
    Work together with your teammates and achieve glorious victory!



    Available to Level 101+, Zero character who has completed Chapter 2 for Story Quest.




    Movement Keys
  • Up: Move Up
  • Down: Move Down
  • Left: Move Left
  • Right: Move Right

    • Kick Ball
  • Press CTRL with the direction key to kick in the direction you were inputting.
  • Holding Ctrl increases the distance of your kick based on how long you held the key.
  • If gauge becomes full, will immediately kick in the direction that the character is facing.
  • If the ball collides with another character while it is moving they will gain control of the ball.
  • Skill Cooldown: 3 Seconds

    • Tackle
  • When the ALT key is pressed, your character will immediately dash in the direction they were moving in.
  • If you collide with another character during the dash, you will perform a tackle.
  • If the ball is in your possession, dashing towards an opponent will not activate the tackle.
  • Opponents who have been tackled are unable to move for 2 sec, and will lose possession of the ball if they had it.
  • Skill Cooldown: 3 Seconds

    • Skill
    (Curve Shot)
  • Pressing Z key will activate curve shot.
  • Can only be used within the half line of the opponent's goal.
  • If gauge is full after holding z, the ball will curve automatically towards the goal.
  • If you fail to fill the gauge up to a certain point, skill will fail to activate and a 3 sec penalty is issued before you can do the skill again.
  • Skill Cooldown: 10 Seconds

    • Skill
    (Power Shot)
  • Press F key twice to activate Power shot.
  • Can only be used within the half line of the opponent's goal.
  • Press once to begin filling the gauge, and press F again when the gauge is in the green region to send the ball flying!
  • When activated, the ball will shoot towards the goal.
  • If you press the F key outside of the green region, the skill will not activate, and a 3 sec penalty will be issued before you can activate the skill again.
  • Skill Cooldown: 10 Seconds

    • Skill
    (Full Power Sprint)
  • Press E key to increase character movement speed by 5 Seconds.
  • Skill Cooldown: 7 Seconds

  • Look for NPC Camila in the event map.
  • Queue up into a 5v5 Red Team vs Blue Team and score goals for 3 minutes.
  • When the game starts, move your character and take the ball by collide your character with the ball.
  • Move towards the opponent's goal post and kick the ball to score a goal by pressing CTRL.
  • Your team gain 1 point every time a goal is scored.
  • The team with the most points wins.
      ↪ Win: 300 Collection Points
      ↪ Draw: 150 Collection Points
      ↪ Lose: 100 Collection Points
  • You can obtained max 300 Collection Points per day per MapleID.
  • You can still play the game even after you have reached the 300 Collection Points daily limit but you will not get anymore Collection Points for the day.
  • 5 sec after game starts, every 35 sec 1 player from each team will be chosen to be able to activate special skills.
  • Kicking the ball into your own team's goal twice will remove you from the mini-game immediately. Furthermore, you will be unable to queue again for 10 minutes.
  • Depending on your total wins, you get cool rewards. (Claim them in the Collection Book.)

    » FAQs

    Q: What is the iGacha?
    A: iGacha is a web based system which contains a totally different set of items and rates from the in-game Gachapon System.

    Q: How to login to iGacha page?
    A: Previously, you would need a Maple iGacha account to login, but now you only need to login your MapleStorySEA Passport account to be able to use the iGacha feature.

    Q: What are Diamonds?
    A: Diamonds are currency required to play the i-Gacha. You will need to purchase Diamonds before you can proceed to play i-Gacha.

    Q: What will happen to my previous M-Coin balance?
    A: If you have any balance, it will be converted to Diamonds and can be used at the new iGacha when there is an active event.

    Q: How do I purchase Diamonds?
    A: After converting your @Cash into Maple Cash, you may convert Maple Cash to Diamonds directly via your MapleStorySEA Passport dashboard conversion UI.

    Q: The i-Gacha machine is out of stock but I still have Diamonds!
    A: Please take note that there are no refunds made for unused Diamonds, nor can they be reverted back to Maple Game Cash. However, the Diamonds can be kept for use in future i-Gacha events. Diamonds do not have any expiry date. With that in mind, please make your conversions wisely.

    Q: Where can I get my item code?
    A: You will be able to view your 30-digit item code in the Storage section of i-Gacha website. You are advised to utilize them before the expiry date which will be listed on the event page.


    Q: How do I redeem my item code(s)?
    A: You can redeem your codes via the Cash Shop in-game. Click the button at the top right corner of the screen and enter your code in the empty fields before clicking on 'OK'.
