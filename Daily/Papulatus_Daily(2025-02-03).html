NEXON Open ID 로그인 기능 추가 안내


你好.
NEXON Open API 입니다.

 

2/3(월) NEXON Open API에 새로운 기능이 도입되어 상세 내용 안내드립니다.

 

 

업데이트 내용
• 이제 NEXON Open API에서 '게임 데이터 활용을 위한 로그인'(NEXON Open ID) 기능이 제공됩니다.
• NEXON Open API를 사용하여 제작된 서비스에서, 넥슨 계정 기반 로그인으로 내 정보를 쉽게 확인할 수 있습니다.

 

기능 적용 대상
• NEXON Open API 메이플스토리 파트너스 제작자인 경우, 해당 기능을 이용할 수 있습니다.
• NEXON Open ID 기능이 보다 안전하게 활용될 수 있도록 서비스 적용 대상을 제한하오니, 이 점 양해 부탁드립니다.

 

기능 적용 후 이용 방법
• '게임 데이터 활용 로그인' 기능이 적용된 서비스에서, '넥슨ID 로그인' 버튼을 클릭 후 넥슨 계정 정보로 로그인
• 넥슨 계정이 있는 모든 이용자가 로그인 진행 가능

 

적용 일시
• 2025년 2월 3일(월)

 

해당 기능에 대한 더 자세한 설명은 기능 소개 페이지를 참고해주시기 바랍니다.

 

많은 관심과 성원 부탁드리며, 더 좋은 서비스로 찾아뵙겠습니다.

谢谢.

    Available to Level 101+, Zero character who has completed Chapter 2 for Story Quest

    Before starting Blue Hole Exploration daily, simply hunt 1,000 level range monsters (-20/+20 Level) and move the right side end of the boat and hold SPACE to start Blue Hole Exploration.



    When exploration is done, simply perform one of the methods below to complete the Blue Hole Exploration with the fishes collected for the day.


  • Use your Movement Key W,A,S,D and swim back up to the surface and hold SPACE.


  • Look for a blue icon above and hold SPACE.


  • Select and press C to use an Emergency Pager obtained from the Exploration Box.

  • However, if you have use up all the oxygen, select Quit and Exit to Boat in your inventory or exceed 1 hour time limit, Blue Hole Exploration will end immediately and lose all your fish collected for that round of expedition.
  • You can try again up to 2 more times if your expedition was unsuccessful.
  • Note that taking damage from fish deducts certain amount of oxygen.

    [Save The Diver] Bancho Sushi Business


    5th February 2025 (After Maintenance) ~ 11th March 2025, 2359hrs



    Available to Level 101+, Zero character who has completed Chapter 2 for Story Quest


    Before Open Restaurant


  • Press 1 to manage your menu and Employee before opening the restaurant to maximize your gold profit.

    MENU
  • At the beginning of progression, Nigiri Sushi and Green Tea will only be available on the menu.


  • As you progress through the event, you can obtain fish meat and turn them into the menu to sell for more Gold.

    Adding Menu
  • Press Add to add more menu.
  • Use your mouse to select the dish you have made with the fish meat and press OK.


  • Select the total quantity you want to be in the menu.
  • You can sell more if you have more of the fish meat.
  • Press the arrow left or right to increase or increase the quantity to be sold and press OK.


  • You can check if the dish is added to the menu.


    Manage Menu
  • In the menu, press Manage to show options.


  • You can either add the quantity, enhance the current dish or remove from the menu


    Enhance Dish
  • In the Menu, press Left Ctrl or use your mouse and click Enhance Dish.


  • You can enhance the dish up to level 5 if you have the fish meat requirement.
  • The higher the dish level, the more Gold when it is sold.



    EMPLOYEE
  • Note: Employees can only be hired when you complete certain Chapter Mission
     ↪1st Kitchen Staff: Clear Chapter 7
     ↪2nd Kitchen Staff: Clear Chapter 10
     ↪1st Hall Staff: Clear Chapter 3
     ↪2nd Hall Staff: Clear Chapter 7
  • There are 2 types of employee category, 1 for Kitchen and 1 for Hall.
  • Up to 2 Kitchen Employees and 2 Hall Employees can assist at a time.
  • Kitchen Staff increase the overall dish preparation speed.
  • Hall Staff assist on serving the customers.
  • Some Employees have special skills that improve their performance.

    Hire Employee
  • Press Left Ctrl or use your mouse and click Hire at the bottom left.


  • The more Gold available, the more expensive you can hire.



  • Select any of the desired employees and click select.


  • You can choose to see the employee details before hiring.



  • After selecting the hire option, a confirmation to hire will be shown.
  • Select Sign to hire the employee.




    Add Employee

  • Click Add employee and click Select.


  • Select the desired employee and click Select to add.


  • Once ready, hold E to Open Restaurant




    Open Restaurant

    Press 1 to manage your menu and Employee before opening the restaurant to maximize your gold profit.

    CUSTOMER
  • When the restaurant is open, customers will enter the shop and choose a dish after a short period.
  • The amount of customers entering the shop will increase when more chapter missions are cleared.
  • Some customers will choose Green Tea first which generates more gold.
  • Some customers will also leave the table dirty after eating a dish.

    Green Tea Serving


  • When a customer wants a Green Tea, a cup icon speech bubble will appear instead of the icon of a dish on the customer.
  • Move near the Green Tea customer and press space to serve Green Tea.
  • A mini game to pour the Green Tea will appear.
  • Hold S to pour the Green Tea .


  • The objective is to pour the Green Tea as close as possible to the Green arrow on the right of the cup.
  • Customers will pay extra gold for Good and Perfect grade while no gold for Bad grade.
  • If the Green Tea overflows or is too little, it is considered a Bad grade.
  • If the customer wants the Green Tea for too long and is not served, the speech bubble will start becoming red.
  • After some time if the red is filled up, the customer will not want the Green Tea anymore and start choosing dishes instead.


    Serving Dish


  • When a customer orders a dish, a dish icon speech bubble will appear on the customer.
  • NPC Bancho and the Kitchen Employee will automatically start preparing the dish.
  • After the dish is complete, move near NPC Bancho to get the dish by pressing SPACE.


  • Once you get the dish, the dish icon speech bubble will appear on your character.
  • Move near to a customer that has the same dish icon and serve the dish by pressing SPACE.
  • If the customer wants the dish for too long and is not served, the speech bubble will start becoming red.
  • After some time if the red is filled up, the customer will not want the dish anymore and leaves the shop immediately.


    Cleaning Plates


  • Sometimes after a customer finish the dish, the customer will leave the seat dirty.
  • You can clean the seat by moving near to the dirty dish icon speech bubble and press SPACE.
  • Press A and D quickly to clean up the seat.


  • Cleaning a dirty seat will gain a small amount of Gold.
  • If the seat is not cleaned before a customer finishes choosing menu item, customer will leave immediately.
  • Customers will look for the empty clean seats first before the dirty seats.
  • Customers sit at the dirty seat will not order Green Tea.

    Grate Wasabi


  • Every dish cost some wasabi to prepare and will deplete overtime.
  • If the wasabi runs out, the dish preparation will stop until it is refilled.
  • To refill wasabi, head to the most right of the map and press space to bring up the wasabi UI.
  • Press W quickly to grate and refill the wasabi up to 100%.
  • The wasabi UI will close automatically when it reaches 100% or press C to close.
