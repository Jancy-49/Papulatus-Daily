  • Junior Designer: Level up to receive the following benefits:
    • Tera Character Burninator: Tradable within world,14-day duration. Use to receive a Tera Burning effect on an existing character between Lv. 10 and 200 for 42 days.

    • (Heroic Worlds) Unique Potential Scroll 100%: Untradable, 7-day duration. 
    • (Interactive Worlds) Equipment Transfer
      • You can transfer a specific equipment item from a character to another one of your characters within the same world!
        • Use the Equipment Transfer UI and select the item to extract. You can preview how the item will be converted before making the decision.
        • Use the ‘Confirm’ button to extract the equipment into an essence. Extracting it into an essence will completely destroy the equipment.
        • Equipment that has been extracted to an essence CANNOT be restored.
        • You can transfer the essence to your character in the same world through Storage.
        • You can preview the changes to the final equipment upon using the essence with the recipient character. The equipment will be changed into appropriate equipment for the class and have the main stats of the recipient character.
      • The appearance and the following stats of the equipment will be adjusted for the recipient character:
        • Bonus Stats
        • Enhanced Stats
        • Potential
        • Bonus Potential (Note: Some Bonus Potentials may not convert into a suitable stat.)
        • Weapon ATT percentage and Magic ATT percentage will be swapped based on the attack stats the recipient character uses.
      • Equipment’s Star Force enhancements and tradeability status will remain the same on the new equipment.
        • If the transferred equipment had ‘cannot be traded when equipped’ tradeability, it will become untradable.
      • You can transfer the following equipment:
        • Fafnir Weapon
        • AbsoLab Weapon (Note: AbsoLab Weapons that cannot be enhanced are excluded.)
        • Lv. 100 Secondary Weapon (Note: Dual Blade's secondary weapons and "Evolving" secondary weapons are excluded.)
        • Gold Emblems (Note: Zero's Eternal Time Emblem is excluded. Angelic Buster, Kaiser, and Xenon’s Emblems are excluded.)
      • Equipment transfer is available once per world.
      • If your recipient character does not claim the transferred equipment within the event duration, the equipment will be lost.
  • Veteran Designer: Level up to receive the following benefits:
    • Hair Transfer
      • You can transfer one of your hairstyles (style and color) to another character within the same world!
      • It can be used by clicking the Special Perks button in the event UI.
        • The hair your character currently has equipped will be extracted so make sure to make sure to check the following: 
          • Character’s gender.
          • Regardless of the color, if the same hair exists in the Beauty Album, it cannot be transferred.
          • Default hair and unisex hair cannot be transferred.
        • When confirming extraction, your character’s hairstyle will be changed to a default hairstyle.
          • Cutie Hair (Female)
          • Toben Hair (Male)
        • After extracting the hairstyle you will be given a Special Hair Transfer Coupon: Tradable within world, 30-day duration.
  • Master Designer: Level up to receive the following benefits:
    • Mount Transfer
      • Extract and transfer a mount skill already registered on a character to another one of your characters within the same world!
      • Use the Mount Skill Transfer UI to extract and transfer the mount.
        • The Mount Transfer UI can be found through the Special Perks button in the event UI menu.
      • Select the desired mount skill registered on the character. Confirm to extract the selected mount skill.
      • After extracting the mount, you will be given a Mount Skill Transfer Certificate: Tradable within world, expires November 19th at 11:59 PM UTC.
      • You can transfer the certificate item to your character in the same world through the Storage.
      • Use it on a recipient character to receive the extracted mount skill.
      • Mount transfer is available once per world.
      • Certain job-exclusive mounts cannot be transferred.
      • If your recipient character does not use the certificate item by November 19th at 11:59 PM UTC, the item will be lost.

Get Your Time Travel Tickets Here!

October 9th (End of Maintenance) - November 19th at 11:59 PM UTC

Past, Present, and Future... Time works differently here! Fill up the Eos Hourglass to get on the FAST track to quick EXP if you don’t have the time OR go SLOW filling the Helios Hourglass for EXP while you hunt for a LONGER time!

Requirement: Lv. 101 and above. (Zero characters must have completed Story Quest Act 2).

  • After completing the ‘[Night Troupe] Get the Night Troupe While It's Hot!’ quest you can access the event UI or talk to Troupe Leader Spiegelmann to participate in one of the following two events each week:
    • [Night Troupe] Eos Hourglass
    • [Night Troupe] Helios Hourglass
  • You can select the desired weekly attraction by clicking on the respective coupon from the Night Troupe Event Ticket UI.
  • Once you’ve made the selection, you CANNOT change your main attraction until the given week ends.
    • The weekly event selection will reset every Wednesday at 12:00 AM UTC during the event duration.
    • You are able to select different quests EACH week.
    • Night Troupe main attraction selection is shared between your characters within the same world.
  • Your perks in each of these attractions will vary based on your Festival Designer Rank.
  • Skill Activation Limits will reset every day at 12:00 AM UTC.
  • Both the Eos Hourglass’ skill and the Helios Hourglass’ skill perks and activation capacity are shared per world.

Eos Hourglass

  • ZIP through time and ZOOM to the future with Spiegelmann!
  • Accept the ‘[Night Troupe] Eos Hourglass’ quest from the Night Troupe Event Ticket UI to receive its perks for the week. Upon making the selection, you will be given an ‘Eos Hourglass’ skill.
  • Each time you defeat monsters near your level (20 levels below and 20 levels above), you will accumulate Time of the Future and fully fill the Hourglass Gauge after 500 monsters are defeated.
  • When the Hourglass Gauge is full, the skill will be activated and you will receive an EXP buff for 150 seconds. The amount of EXP obtained varies depending on your Designer Rank.
    • Junior: 350%
    • Senior: 400%
    • Veteran: 450%
    • Master: 500%
  • The Eos Hourglass skill will be deactivated once you’ve used the max number of daily activations.
    • Daily activation capacity is limited to 5 times per world.

Helios Hourglass

  • Take it LOW and SLOW and CHILL in the past with Spiegelmann!
  • Accept the ‘[Night Troupe] Helios Hourglass’ quest from the Night Troupe Event Ticket UI to receive its perks for the week. Upon making the selection, you will be given a ‘Helios Hourglass’ skill.
  • Each time you defeat monsters near your level (20 levels below and 20 levels above), you will accumulate Time of the Past and fully fill the Hourglass Gauge after 500 monsters are defeated.
  • When the Hourglass Gauge is full, the skill will be activated and you will receive an EXP buff for 150 seconds. The amount of EXP obtained varies depending on your Designer Rank.
    • Junior: 200%
    • Senior: 250%
    • Veteran: 300%
    • Master: 350%
  • The Helios Hourglass skill will be deactivated once you’ve used the max number of daily activations.
    • Daily activation capacity is limited to 25 times per world.
