    Champion Burning Double Up


    1 + 1 + 1

    25th December 2024, 1000hrs ~ 11th March 2025, 2359hrs



    Available to Level 101+, Zero character who has completed Chapter 2 for Story Quest


    ❗What is Champion Double Up?
    Champion Double Up allows you to designate three Champion characters in a World. One of them can be designated as the Champion Leader first.

    Double Up Attendance
    🔹 Double Up Attendance can only be performed by the Champion Leader by hunting monsters within the level range.
    🔹 Each Champion will receive 2,500 Double Up Coins for each completed attendance. A dedicated Coin Shop is available for each Champion character.
    (7,500 total for each attendance)

    Double Up EXP Points Accumulation
    🔹 Double Up EXP Points can only be accumulated by the Champion Leader.
    🔹 Every time the Leader hunts a monster within the level range, the non-Leader Champions' Double Up EXP Points will increase (up to 100,000 per week).
    🔹 You can log in to your non-Leader Champions to spend Double Up EXP Points and earn EXP.

    Note
    🔹 You can only continue in the same World where you initially assigned the Leader.
    🔹 The designated Champions can never be changed to another character.
    🔹 You can only change your Leader once a week from among your designated Champions.
  • Once understood, select I understand now. Go Assign a leader..

    CHAMPION DOUBLE UP


    IMPORTANT GUIDE

    Setting Leader
    1. Click Set in the Leader Box.


    2. Choose the desired leader character and click SELECT.


    3. You will be shown a confirmation if you want to select the current login world to participate the event.


    4. Type the (Current World)/Proceed.


    5. Click Yes if the desired character selection is final. If not, click No, to change.


    6. After confirming the character, type (IGN)/Proceed.


    Setting Champion
    1. Click Set in the Champion Box.


    2. Choose the desired champion character and click SELECT.


    3. You will be shown a confirmation if you want the selected character to be set as Champion.


    4. After confirming the character, type (IGN)/Proceed.


  • LEADER will have a special skill located in the beginner tab to summon both Champions.


    IMPORTANT NOTE
     ↪ You cannot change the LEADER and CHAMPIONS after assigning them.
     ↪ You cannot set the same character as both LEADER and CHAMPIONS.
     ↪ You can only change the LEADER from either of the selected CHAMPIONS.
     ↪ Hyper Burning Characters CAN be selected.

    General Progression
  • Eliminate level range monsters (-20/+20) with LEADER to gain points for both Champion.
  • up to 100,000 monsters per week which resets after Wednesday 11:59p.m.
  • 1 level range monsters (-20/+20) = 1 Point.
  • Eliminate level range monsters (-20/+20) with CHAMPION will not gain any points.
  • Points obtained by both Champion can be converted into EXP points.
  • EXP Points gained will be based on the character's level.


    Double Up Coin
  • Eliminate 2,000 level range monsters (-20/+20) with LEADER 3 times a week to get up to 7,500 Double Up Coins.
  • Both Champion will obtain its own separate Double Up Coins along with the LEADER.
  • Up to 100,000 level range monsters (-20/+20) can be eliminated by the LEADER per week which resets after Wednesday 11:59p.m.
  • Click the USE button to consume 3,000 Maple Points for DOUBLE UP PASS to catch up with the times you missed.
  • Both LEADER and the 2 CHAMPION will obtain its own Double Up Coins from the DOUBLE UP PASS.

    [Coin Shop] Champion Double Up Coin Shop

  • Both LEADER and the 2 CHAMPION has its own purchase limit that does not affect each other.
