The Marvel Machine Returns!


Marvel Machine is back for a limited time with a brand-new look! If you have never played before, this is a game which allows you to play for in-game equipment and Cash items from the MapleStory website, now available for the first time on your mobile devices as well. Each Marvel that you purchase with NX will reward you with three random prizes! You never know, you might win one of the exclusive items only available from Marvel Machine!

What prizes lie hidden within the Marvel Machine? We have added several new items such as:
  • Cutie Trio Chair, Fireworks Festival Chair, Desert Rabbit Sofa Chair, Peek-A-Boo Mushroom Chair, Cat-in-a-Box Chair, Maplegotchi chair, Sun Chair, and Pink Bean Ice Cream Umbrella chair
  • Commander Magician's Ring Coupon: A mysterious ring that decreases MP costs by half when equipped. Coupon has an unlimited duration, resulting ring has a 90-day duration.
  • Scroll Fuser: Can be used to combine five of the same X, Evolution, or 9th Anniversary Prime scrolls into a new, random X Scroll.
  • Star Force +1-Star Enhancement Scroll 30% and Star Force +1-Star Enhancement Scroll 50%
  • Scroll for V One-Handed Weapon for ATT 100%, Scroll for V One-Handed Weapon for Magic ATT 100%, Scroll for V Two-Handed Weapon for ATT 100%, Scroll for V Two-Handed Weapon for Magic ATT 100%, Scroll for V Armor for ATT 100%, Scroll for V Armor for Magic ATT 100%, Scroll for V Accessory for ATT 100%, Scroll for V Accessory for Magic ATT 100%, Scroll for V Pet Equip. for ATT 100%, and Scroll for V Pet Equip. for Magic ATT 100%!
    • V 100% Scrolls have a 100% chance to increase the ATT or Magic ATT (depending on the scroll) of the selected item by +6 to +8.

For this run of the Marvel Machine, weâ€™ve removed the following: Corgi Rump Trampoline Chair, Report Received Chair, Teddy Bear Kitchen Window Chair, I'm Catnip chair, Mushroom Shrine Chair, Lucid's Jewel Box Chair, Feline Felicity chair, and Pup Tent Chair; Beast Tamer Transparent Ears and Transparent Tail; 9th Anniversary Prime Scrolls for One-Handed Weapon, Two-Handed Weapon, Armor, and Accessory; Evolution Scrolls for One-handed Weapon ATT, One-handed Weapon Magic ATT, Two-handed Weapon ATT, and Two-handed Weapon Magic ATT.

The Marvel Machine also has three Cubes to help get your gear good and strong! The Glowing Cube and Bonus Glowing Cubes can be used on Rare to Legendary items to reroll Potential. The Bright Cube can be used on Rare to Legendary items to reroll Potential with the option to choose before or after Potential lines, and can increase up to 2 ranks according to set probability rates.

You can also still get your hands on items such as Violet Cubes!

The Violet Cube gives you TWICE the lines of your current number of Potential lines to choose from. If your item has two lines of Potentials, you will get to pick two new from a list of four Potential options. If it had three lines of Potentials, you will get to pick three new from a list of six Potential options. This cube helps you to obtain the type of Potential you want even faster!

We've also brought back the highly sought after Battle-Roid Coupon (which grants you a male or female Battle-Roid), Firestarter Ring Coupon (which gives a ring that grants a skill that can be activated to increase the Burning stage level of the current field), Ring of Torment Coupon (which gives a ring that reduces the player's damage dealt by 30% while granting a 15% EXP bonus), and Lucid's Earrings Coupon (which gives earrings that allow you to frighten your enemies with nightmares, stunning 15 enemies for 9 seconds).

These items are available too: Magnificent Lucid Soul, Permanent Hyper Teleport Rock Coupon (tradeable), and Transparent Equipment Covers.

You can still get your hands on returning favorites like the Breath of Divinity Ring, Reaper's Pendant, Lv. 150 Tower of Oz equipment (Ryude's Sword, Alicia's Mutated Staff, Lightseeker, and Aquarius Crown), Lv. 150 Fafnir weapons, Lv. 160 Terminus weapons, Lv. 160 Sweetwater equipment, Lv. 200 Arcane Umbra equipment, Lightning God Ring, Cursed Kaiserium, Dragon Khanjar, Android Hearts (Outlaw Heart, Lidium Heart, Superior Lidium Heart, Titanium Heart, and Electronic Heart O-Series), Marvelous medal, and Tinkerer's Chest!

Or score a new outfit with Seasonal Surprise Style Boxes including the community favorite, the Anniversary Surprise Style Box! Valentine, Black Mage and Halloween boxes are also available.
