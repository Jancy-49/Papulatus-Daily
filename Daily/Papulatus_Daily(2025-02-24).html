Unscheduled Maintenance - February 24, 2025


[Updated at 8:30 AM PDT] The maintenance has been completed and we have extended Cash Shop items in your inventory for 3 hours. Please restart your Nexon Launcher or Steam to make sure that you get the latest client! Thank you for your patience.

Hi Maplers,

We will be performing an unscheduled maintenance today that we expect to last approximately 3 hours. Information and details will be shared soon. We appreciate your patience and understanding at this time.

We will begin maintenance today, February 24, 2025 at 6:30 AM PST (9:30 AM EST / 3:30 PM CET / 1:30 AM AEDT February 25).

Thank you for your cooperation,

The MapleStory Team

  • What will be unavailable:
    • All MapleStory game servers.

Addressing the Recent Unicube Issues


Hello Maplers,

We apologize for the delayed response regarding the Unicube issue that occurred on February 22nd in Interactive Worlds. We have been reviewing the Unicube usage logs, examining the list of fraudulent users, and examining the list of items that were abnormally enhanced. We ask for your understanding as this analysis takes time to ensure an accurate response and resolution.

Below is our planned course of action:

  1. In order to prevent the further spread of problematic items on the server, we will be temporarily withdrawing abnormally enhanced items during today’s emergency maintenance.
  2. After thorough investigation, additional measures will be taken against users who have misused the Unicube.
  3. Once we’ve completed the investigation, compensation for the emergency maintenance will be provided.
  4. Retrieved items will be restored. If needed, there may be additional measures taken such as rolling items back to their state prior to Unicube usage and returning Unicubes where appropriate. 

We are committed to strengthening our internal development processes to prevent such incidents from occurring again and to minimize any inconvenience for our MapleStory players. Once again, we sincerely apologize for any inconvenience caused.

- The MapleStory Team
