    Have a question? Check here to see if your question is answered! Still have a question? Do contact us via iBox for us to check on it! We will update the FAQ from time to time, so do check back!

    Question: Can I attend the event without purchasing a ticket?
    Answer: Yes! The event is free to attend. Feel free to join us for a trip down memory lane. However, Maplers who RSVP will enjoy a more complete experience.

    Question: Can I purchase multiple Basic RSVP or Premium RSVP?
    Answer: You are allowed to purchase up to two (2) of each RSVP Pack. The system is actively monitored to prevent abuse.

    Question: I have purchased the RSVP Packs but cannot make it to the event. Can I ask someone to redeem it on my behalf?
    Answer: You may allow someone else to redeem it on your behalf at your discretion. However, we encourage you to redeem your RSVP pack and enjoy the event yourself for the best experience. The Event Organizers are not responsible for any lost tickets in such cases.

    Question: I have received an item from the Stage Events. Can I exchange it for in-game rewards?
    Answer: All rewards from the event are non-exchangeable.

    Question: Is there an expiry for the in-game item codes?
    Answer: All codes related to the Victoria Voyage: Sailing Beyond event can be redeemed until 30th September, 2025, 2359hrs (GMT +8). Please redeem all codes as soon as possible. If you encounter any faulty codes, contact iBox within two weeks of item distribution for prompt support and assistance.

    Question: How many Victoria Voyage Lucky Draw chances can I get if I purchased one RSVP pack?
    Answer: You can receive 1 chance per Basic RSVP Package and 3 per Premium RSVP Package.

    Question: I have won physical merchandise through stage activities. Can I exchange it for in-game rewards or other merchandise?
    Answer: All rewards from the event are non-exchangeable. In case of discrepancies, the event staff's decision will take precedence.

    Question: What if I won the Lucky Draw but was not at the event during the draw time to receive my prize? Will it be forfeited?
    Answer: You must be present during the Lucky Draw segment at 8:00 PM to receive Lucky Draw prizes for the Sailing Beyond event!

    Question: Is the venue pet-friendly?
    Answer: Pets are not allowed at the venue for safety and hygiene reasons. Unless you have a pet rock, please leave your pets at home.

    9th July 2025, (After Maintenance) ~ 19th August 2025, 2359hrs

    Developer Notes

    The Abyssal Expedition team has made its return to El Nath. Begin your expedition by enlisting mercenaries to battle through waves of monsters and gain experience as they progress.
    As your mercenaries grow stronger, you’ll face increasingly powerful bosses. Overcome these challenges to claim the final reward—The Abyssal Expedition Union Block.



    Available to Level 200+, Zero character who has completed Chapter 2 for Story Quest



    To start of your expedition, click the Maple Expedition button to bring up the UI.
    Double click on any of the mercenaries to add them to your team.



    Achieve the playtime daily and receive Expedition Supplies.
    As you increase your Command Rank, you will unlock more slots for mercenaries and better Expedition Supplies



    Once your mercenaries are set, use the portal at the left side of the map to start your journey!



    Depending on the level of the map, you can either enter the left white portal for grinding exp or the orange portal in the map for boss.
    As your mercenaries get stronger, you unlock maps with stronger monsters and bosses.



    Let the mercenaries do the job for you as they will attack the monsters automatically.
    Obtain EXP when monsters has been defeated.
    You can only stay on the map for a set duration.
    As you get stronger, you unlock more maps and stay on the map longer up to 8 hours daily.
