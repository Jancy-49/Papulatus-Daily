    CHASER II: New Name Auction Highlight Notes (v238)




    Developer Notes

    Here’s your chance to win the name you’ve always wanted—the New Name Auction is here!

    The event features two phases: Season 1 - New Name Auction Official Bidding and Season 2 - Character Name Buying/Selling.

    Don’t miss out the New Name’s Auction and change to a cool and unique name for your character!


    CAUTIONARY NOTES UPON STARTING

    Notes on Trading
  • MapleStory retains ownership of the character name, and the rights to use the name are transferred to the buyer upon completion of the trade.
  • Character names that violate the Name Policy are subject to Admin judgement, and purchasing and using them may result incur penalties based on the Operation Policy.
  • You can only have 1 listing for sale at a time.
  • You can have up to 10 character name trades going on at a time.
  • When you register to sell a name, the registration deposit and a renaming free will be deducted, and the character will immediately take on a new name.
  • The registration deposit will be refunded if the sale is successful, but not if the sale is unsuccessful.
  • The remaining fee cannot be returned regardless of the success of the sale.
  • If the sale is successful, you will receive the sale proceeds minus the sale fee.
  • If the sale is unsuccessful, you can get your character's name back.

    Notes on Names
  • If a name you list for sale is found to be in violation of the Name Policy, the trade may be canceled by the Admin.
  • Names that violate the Name Policy can be restricted from use and forcibly changed.
  • Name change fees and bid deposits used to trade names that violate the Name Policy are non-refundable.
  • Accounts that trade names in violation of the Name Policy will be restricted from future New Name Auctions.

    Notes on Claiming (Changing) Names
  • You cannot change to a name that already exists.
  • You cannot change to a name that contains certain restricted words.
  • Changing a character's name will not change the name on previously crafted items.
  • NPCs registered as statues cannot be renamed.


    CAUTIONARY NOTES WHEN BIDDING

    Trade-Related Cautionary Notes
  • Ownership of character names belongs to MapleStory, and upon successful trade, naming rights are transferred to the buyer.
  • Character names that violate the Name Policy are subject to Admin judgement. Purchasing and using such names may incur penalties based on the Operation Policy.
  • Cancellation due to a change of mind after bidding is not possible. You can rebid at a higher price before the auction ends.
  • A maximum of 4 character name transactions can be conducted simultaneously. This includes both sales and bids, with a limit of 5, including unreceived transactions from sales, bids, and completed deals.
  • Bid deposits and name change fees are deducted in advance when placing a bid, and both are refunded in case of failure.
  • Upon winning, the character name is immediately changed and the previous character name is deleted. Once the character name change is completed, it cannot be reversed.

    Name-Related Cautionary Notes
  • If the bid name is found violate naming policies, the trade may be forcibly canceled.
  • Names violating naming policies may be restricted from use and forcibly changed.
  • Name change fees and bid deposits used in transactions involving names violating policies are non-refundable.
  • Accounts involved in trading names that violate policies may face restrictions on future New Name Auction usage.

    Cautionary Notes for Name Reception (Change) after Winning
  • After receiving the character name, the character cannot change names for one week.
  • The name displayed on items crafted prior to a character name change will not be altered.
  • The names of NPCs registered in the beauty salon will not be changed.


    CAUTIONARY NOTES FOR NAME RECEPTION

    Cautionary Notes for Name Reception
  • After receiving the character name, the character cannot change name through NPC Mister New Name for one week.
  • The name displayed on items crafted prior to a character name change will not be altered.
  • The names of NPCs registered in the beauty salon will not be changed.
  • Regardless of the timing of the character name change, you can receive character names won through a successful bid or return character names you did not win.



    Season 1 - New Name Auction Official Bidding

    18th December 2024, (after v238 Patch) ~ 7th January 2025, 2359hrs



    Available to Level 10+, Zero character who has completed Chapter 2 for Story Quest.

  • Selected names will be available for purchase using Maple Points.
  • Starting bid for each Name is 1,000 Maple Points.
  • Everyday at 10:00a.m, new names will be available for bidding.
  • Each Name will be in the Auction for 48 hours for bidding starting from the initial time it is added to the Auction.
  • There is a maximum of 10 Trade slots per MapleID.
  • The Filter Function above can be used to search for more specific name details such as Words Only or Numbers only as well as the length and price of the Name.
  • The auction of a Name ends 2 minutes before the designated auction end time, and the highest bid price at the end wins the character Name.
  • The New Name Auction is available on a per Maple ID basis.
  • List of available names can be found HERE.

    How to bid
    1. Click on 'New Name Auction Season 1' from the event list or NPC Mister New Name located in main cities, then click the [JOIN] button to enter the New Name Auction.
    2. Search for the desired character name in the search bar on the top left, or find the 'CHARACTER NAME FOR SALE' you like in the search tab.


    3. Write down your desired bid and bid
      ↪The first bid(1,000 Maple Points) is unaffected by the Minimum Bid Increment and will be available from 1,000 Maple points. Subsequent bids will be affected by the Minimum Bid Increment.
      ↪When bidding, the bid deposit and name change fee are deducted. If the bid is unuccessful, they are returned.
      ↪Cancellation is not possible after bidding.
    4. You can check the names currently being bid on by pressing the [ON SALE] button in the 'MY TRADE' tab.


    5. In the 'BID INFORMATION' tab, if the amount you have bid is the highest for that Name, it will be displayed as the WINNING BID. If someone else has bid a higher amount for a character name that has not yet finished its sales period, the 'RE-BID' and 'RETURN BID AMOUNT' buttons will be activated.
      ↪When re-bidding, the original bid amount will not be returned, but only the added amount is deducted from your Maple Point balance.


    6. If you win a bid, you can receive the name won in the 'TRANSACTION COMPLETED' button under 'MY TRADE' tab. If you receive the name, the name of the currently logged-in character will be changed immediately and the existing character name will be deleted.
      ↪After receiving the character name, the character cannot change their character name through NPC Mister New Name for a week.
      ↪After receiving the character name, you can change it to another name that you have won. However, as the previous name cannot be restored once the change is complete, please check the character that will use the name you have won is correct before receiving the name.
      ↪The name displayed on items previously created by the character whose name you want to change cannot be changed.
      ↪NPCs registered as statues cannot have their name changed.

    New Name Auction

    Available January 15 (after maintenance) - February 11 at 11:59 PM UTC

    Requirement: Lv. 10 and above characters (Zero characters that completed Story Quest Act 2).

    Ready to trade names? Want to snag that perfect character name or sell your old one with Maple Points?

    • You can enter the New Name Auction by selecting 'New Name Auction' from the Event List and clicking ‘Participate’ or you can speak with Mr. Newname, located in major towns like Henesys.
    • You will be able to make transactions from Interactive and Heroic!
    • Completed trade history is stored until June 10 at 11:59 PM UTC. Names and Maple Points from sales that aren't claimed during the storage period will disappear so make sure you claim them before then!
    • You can have up to 5 character name transactions (selling or bidding).
    • Now, let’s start off by reviewing some major auction terms that you’ll need know before you begin!

    Auction Terms

    Description

    Bid

    The act of writing and entering the desired amount by one who wishes to purchase a character name for sale.

    Minimum Bid

    The desired price that the seller wrote when registering the character name.

    Current Bid

    The desired price the buyer enters by raising the bid by at least the minimum bid amount.

    Successful Bid

    The trade is completed if the bid submitted by the buyer is the highest amount at the end of the sale.

    Failed Bid, Unsuccessful Bid

    Your bid gets canceled because someone else made a successful bid with an amount higher than the 'Current Bid' that you entered.

    Registration Deposit

    The amount automatically deducted when you start selling character names, which is 5% of the minimum bid.

    Name Change Fee

    Amount paid to Mr. Newname when changing character names.
    • Now that you know the terminology, we can go over how to sell and bid on character names.

    How to Sell Character Names:

    • Enter the New Name Auction and start by entering your desired bid, duration, and a new character name.
      • Please note that character names that have started selling cannot be canceled or changed midway.
      • The minimum bid starts at 50,000 Maple Points, and the registration deposit and name change fee are deducted at the time of registration.

    • You can keep tabs of your current sale of character names by clicking the [Selling] button in the ‘Your Trades’ tab.
    • When the character name sale is finished, you can check the results by clicking the [End Trade] button in the 'Your Trades' tab.
      • Transaction fees of 30% are deducted at the time of payment collection.
      • Registration deposits are refunded if the sale is successful.
      • If the registered character name was not sold, you can pay an additional registration deposit and re-list the name for sale or you can either return or claim your name back.
      • Name change fees are non-refundable regardless of whether the sale was successful or not.
      • You can claim your payment for the sold character name by clicking the [Collect] button in the ‘End Trade’ tab.
    • Character names that were won while the New Name Auction was open, cannot be re-sold during the event period. 

    How to Purchase Character Names:

    • Need to get a hold of that name? Head to the ‘New Name Auction’ UI and search for the character name you want in the search bar at the top left, or you can look through other name options from the ‘Character Names for Sale’ from the search tab.
    • Enter your desired bid amount and submit.
      • The bidding price must be increased by at least the minimum bid amount.
      • If your bid is unsuccessful, both the bid deposit and the name change fee will be refunded to you. Although, you will not be able to cancel after placing your bid.
      • You can keep tabs of your current character bids by clicking the [Bidding] button in the ‘Your Trades’ tab.
    • In the ‘Bidding’ tab, you can see the current highest bid for each available character name. If your bid is the highest, it will be labeled 'Highest Bid.' If another player bids higher, you can either increase your bid or back out.
    • If you have won the Highest Bid, you can claim the name in the ‘End Trade’ tab.
      • Upon claiming the character name, your currently logged-in character's name will be immediately updated. The previous name will be permanently removed.
      • After your name change, you'll have to wait a week before you can change your new name with Mr. Newname.
      • Please note that once the change is complete, you cannot restore your previous name.

    Please note: The New Name Auction is unavailable in Burning World and character names that break the naming policy will have their transaction forcefully cancelled or changed. The name change fees and deposits will not be refunded. You can find other 'New Name Auction' related notes in the 'Please check all cautions' tab.

    내 캐릭터와 사진 찍기, '메이플스토리' 포토존 4주간 운영


    자료제공 - 넥슨


    넥슨(공동 대표 강대현∙김정욱)은 나의 ‘메이플스토리’ 캐릭터와 함께 사진을 찍을 수 있는 포토존을 CGV용산아이파크몰에 마련했다고 17일 밝혔다.

    CGV용산아이파크몰과 ‘메이플스토리’ 이용자의 재미있는 경험을 목적으로 열리는 이번 포토존은 오는 19일 ‘메이플스토리’의 올 겨울 첫 업데이트를 기념해 진행되는 것으로, 지난 7일 쇼케이스 현장에서 나의 캐릭터를 활용한 이색적인 참여형 이벤트로 큰 호응을 얻었던 ‘호텔 아르크스, 버려진 자동차 극장’의 캐릭터 포토존과 동일한 형태로 운영된다.

    CGV용산아이파크몰 7층에 3면으로 된 LED 디스플레이 ‘윙 파노라마’ 공간을 활용, 방문객들에게 내 캐릭터가 오프라인 공간에 등장하는 색다른 경험을 제공한다. 포토존은 내년 1월 15일까지 매일 오후 1시와 4시, 6시에 1시간씩 세 타임으로 운영된다.

    기본적으로는 ‘메이플스토리’ 직업 대표 코디의 캐릭터가 화면에 노출되고, 현장에 온 방문객이 ‘메이플 포토존’ 하단에 설치돼 있는 리더기에 QR 코드를 찍으면 나의 캐릭터가 화면에 등장한다.

    내 캐릭터를 포토존에 노출시키기 위해서는 ‘메이플스토리’ 캐릭터를 보유해야 하며, QR 코드는 앱 ‘메이플핸즈+’에서 확인할 수 있다. 내 캐릭터의 QR 코드가 보이지 않을 경우에는 앱의 최신 업데이트가 필요하다.

    한편, 넥슨 ‘메이플스토리’는 오는 19일 6차 전직 ‘HEXA스킬’의 3차∙4차 마스터리 코어를 추가한다. ‘HEXA스킬’의 4개 마스터리 코어를 게임에 모두 적용하면, 이용자들은 한 층 더 강력해진 캐릭터의 스킬을 즐길 수 있다.
