[Updated 3/06] v.257 Known Issues


Hi Maplers,

The Known Issues section focuses on the major issues that have significantly impacted the game and community, and which our team is actively addressing. Please note that while we aim to cover the most impactful issues, this list will not encompass every reported issue. Check back regularly for updates on the status of these issues and information on resolved issues, or any new issues that may have been added.

Here you’ll find a list of known and resolved issues from the v.257 - Tallahart: Grave of the Gods update:

Known Issues:

  • Champion Burning Challenge
    • The Champion Burning Challenge UI is incorrectly showing Darknell as available to challenge a week early. Defeating Darknell will not be counted until the challenge officially starts on March 12 at 12:00 AM UTC.
  • Common
    • Issue with the skill description of Blaze Wizard's Savage Flame skill.

Resolved Issues:

  • February 25, 2025
    • Resolved an issue where Kazax's Energy Fragment obtained from Althea's Mapae Fragment would sometimes give incorrect rewards in Heroic Worlds.
      1. For players who obtained and used the Chains of Resentment (30 Days) Coupon will have a Kazax's Energy Fragment returned to them in a future maintenance.
      2. For players who obtained but have not used the Chains of Resentment (30 Days) Coupon will have the coupon removed and a Kazax's Energy Fragment returned to them in a future maintenance.
      3. For players who obtained and used a 50% Item Drop Rate Increase Potion, please contact Player Support to receive one Kazax's Energy Fragment.
      4. For players who obtained but have not used a 50% Item Drop Rate Increase Potion, please contact Player Support to have it replaced with a 200% Item Drop Rate Increase Potion.
  • February 20, 2025
    • Resolved an issue where Inspector Coin coupons could not be used in the Ride or Die shop.
  • February 13, 2025
    • Resolved an issue where the Explorer job 'The Suspicious Hat Guy' quest could not be completed.
