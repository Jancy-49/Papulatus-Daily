  • 확률 정보 조회의 ‘잠재능력 재설정 이용 결과 조회’, ‘큐브 사용 결과 조회’의 실시간 조회가 가능하도록 변경됩니다.

적용 일시

  • 2025년 3월 27일(목) 오전 11시 적용

[Updated 3/26] Scheduled Minor Patch - March 27, 2025


[Updated 11:35 PM PDT] The maintenance has been completed and we have extended Cash Shop items in your inventory for 7 hours. Please restart your Nexon Launcher or Steam to make sure that you get the latest client! Thank you for your patience.

  • Items lost due to the Steam Community Market issue have been returned to your Steam Inventory.

  • After further investigation, we will be compensating players for the amount of all types of cubes used on items affected by the issue. For additional information on the Unicube Issue please see our post here.
