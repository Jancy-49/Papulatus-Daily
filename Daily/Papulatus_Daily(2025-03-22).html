Steam Community Market (Beta) Issue Update


Hi Maplers,

We’re excited to see everyone’s interest in the launch of the Steam Community Market (Beta) feature! However, we’re aware that a small number of players experienced an issue where their items disappeared when attempting to register them to the Steam Inventory UI.

As we continue to improve and refine this Beta feature, we’ve identified the cause and are working on a fix scheduled for next week’s maintenance.

To prevent further issues, we're temporarily disabling the ability to register items to the Steam Inventory UI and the sale of Oswell's Scissors of Karma today, at 2:00 PM PDT (5:00 PM EDT / 10:00 PM CET / 8:00 AM AEDT March 22), until the fix is in place. Please note that any items already in your Steam Inventory will continue to function normally on the Steam Community Market and can be retrieved as usual.

For players who were affected, any lost items will be returned once the issue is resolved.

We appreciate your patience and sincerely apologize for the inconvenience.

Thank you,

The MapleStory Team

  • A Dream Luna quality Somi 'n Sae, Mung 'n Moon, Nyan 'n Kham, Winter Deer, Snow Ram, Winter Snow, Power XO Bun, Super XO Bun, and Magic XO Bun 90-day pet.
  • Better chances at a Petite Luna quality Demon Slayer the anime Lil Tanjiro, Lil Nezuko, and Lil Zenitsu 90-day pet.
