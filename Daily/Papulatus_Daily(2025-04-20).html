[Updated Apr] Terry's 20th Anniversary Gifts!




Developer Notes

2025 marks the 20th anniversary of MapleSEA! To celebrate, we’ve planned a special event to offer you a meaningful gift on the 20th of each month.

Enjoy a variety of rewards and take a nostalgic journey through your MapleStory adventures so far.

We’ve prepared something extra special in June, the month of the actual anniversary. Please look forward to it and make sure to claim your reward on the 20th of every month!


1st January 2025, 1000hrs ~ 31st December 2025, 2359hrs

Available to Level 101+, Zero character who has completed Chapter 2 for Story Quest.
Quest can be performed ONCE PER MAPLEID only.


  • NPC Terry in a 20 Birthday suit can be found in Henesys, Nameless Village and Cernium.
  • At the 20th day of every month in 2025, look for NPC Terry in a 20 Birthday suit to receive a special gift.
  • Time to claim at every 20th of the month:
      ↪ 20th January 2025 from 12:00a.m. to 11:59p.m.
      ↪ 20th February 2025 from 12:00a.m. to 11:59p.m.
      ↪ 20th March 2025 from 12:00a.m. to 11:59p.m.
      ↪ 20th April 2025 from 12:00a.m. to 11:59p.m.
      ↪ 20th May 2025 from 12:00a.m. to 11:59p.m.
      ↪ 20th June 2025 from 12:00a.m. to 11:59p.m.
      ↪ 20th July 2025 from 12:00a.m. to 11:59p.m.
      ↪ 20th August 2025 from 12:00a.m. to 11:59p.m.
      ↪ 20th September 2025 from 12:00a.m. to 11:59p.m.
      ↪ 20th October 2025 from 12:00a.m. to 11:59p.m.
      ↪ 20th November 2025 from 12:00a.m. to 11:59p.m.
      ↪ 20th December 2025 from 12:00a.m. to 11:59p.m.




    GENERAL INFO

  • Look for NPC Terry in a 20 Birthday suit and click the NPC to accept the quest [20th Anniversary] Today is Gift Day: (Month).
  • An NPC dialogue will popup to request you to key in the Character Name/World name/Receive to confirm receive the gift for all "Terry's 20th Anniversary Gift Box: (Month)".
  • You will not be able to receive the gift if you type in incorrectly.
  • General Rewards for Every Terry's 20th Anniversary Gift Box of the month.


    Sol Erda Fragment Coupon x200


    Prepared Pendant of the Spirit Coupon (30 Days)
    (Gives additional +30% EXP)

  • An additional special skill Today is 20th Anniversary Gift Day! will be given for the whole day of 20th of every month!
  • Today is 20th Anniversary Gift Day! skill provides an Additional EXP Obtained +20%.
  • Today is 20th Anniversary Gift Day! skill last from 12:00a.m. to 11:58p.m. at every 20th of the month.
  • Today is 20th Anniversary Gift Day! skill can be toggle On/Off by using Right-click from your mouse.






    20th January 2025

    Event Duration: 20th January 2025 from 12:00a.m. to 11:59p.m.


    QUEST: [20th Anniversary] Today is Gift Day: January



    Dreamy Bear Set


    QUEST: [20th Anniversary] Maple's Old Friend: January
  • Look for NPC Chief Stan near NPC Terry in a 20 Birthday suit and click the NPC to accept the quest [20th Anniversary] Maple's Old Friend: January.
  • NPC Chief Stan will give Blue Snail Shell x100 as gift.
  • Blue Snail Shell from NPC Chief Stan is a USE item which can be thrown at monsters to deal 20 Damage.






    20th February 2025

    Event Duration: 20th February 2025 from 12:00a.m. to 11:59p.m.


    QUEST: [20th Anniversary] Today is Gift Day: February



    Karma Choice Cube
    (Quantity: 20)


    QUEST: [20th Anniversary] Maple's Old Friend: February
  • Look for NPC Joel Stan near NPC Terry in a 20 Birthday suit and click the NPC to accept the quest [20th Anniversary] Maple's Old Friend: February.
  • NPC Joel Stan will give an Orbis Bound Ticket as a gift.


  • Orbis Bound Ticket from NPC Joel Stan is an item to add songs to Victoria Island CD Player from [My Home] content that you can listen to on the flight to Orbis.
  • Song Title Added:
      ↪ Upon the Sky
      ↪ Arab Pirates!




    20th March 2025

    Event Duration: 20th March 2025 from 12:00a.m. to 11:59p.m.


    QUEST: [20th Anniversary] Today is Gift Day: March




    SEA Royal Face Coupon Ticket
    (Quantity: 10)
    Face Look refer to 19 March 2025 Cash Shop Update



    Mysterious Mix Color Contact Lens Coupon Ticket
    (Quantity: 10)


    QUEST: [20th Anniversary] Maple's Old Friend: March
  • Look for NPC Adobis near NPC Terry in a 20 Birthday suit and click the NPC to accept the quest [20th Anniversary] Maple's Old Friend: March.
  • NPC Adobis will give an Zakum's Throne as a gift.






    20th April 2025

    Event Duration: 20th April 2025 from 12:00a.m. to 11:59p.m.


    QUEST: [20th Anniversary] Today is Gift Day: April



    Maximum Growth Potion
    (Quantity: 3)


    QUEST: [20th Anniversary] Maple's Old Friend: April
  • Look for NPC Shane near NPC Terry in a 20 Birthday suit and click the NPC to accept the quest [20th Anniversary] Maple's Old Friend: April.
  • NPC Shane will give an Dried Double-Rooted Red Ginseng as a gift.





    Terry's Gift for the remaining months will be released at the stroke of the 20th midnight~
