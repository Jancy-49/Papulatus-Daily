의문의 결계 이벤트 오류 수정 및 복구 안내





你好. 这里是冒险岛.


의문의 결계 이벤트에서 결계 제어봉 보유 가능 최대치인 20개를 이미 보유한 경우에는 결계 제어봉이 지급되지 않는 것이 정상이나,
특정 상황에서 결계 제어봉 일일 지급량을 받을 수 있는 오류가 수정됩니다.


해당 오류를 경험한 勇士们 결계 제어봉을 ‘의문의 결계’ 이벤트 시작 시점부터 현재까지

획득 가능한 결계 제어봉의 최대치보다 더 많이 받아갈 수 있는 상황은 아니었습니다.


또한, 결계 제어봉은 최대 20개까지 보유할 수 있기 때문에 보유량에 따라서 3개 미만의 일일 결계 제어봉을 획득하는 것은 정상적인 상황이나,
정상적으로 3개 미만의 결계 제어봉을 지급받은 勇士们께
오류를 경험했다면 받을 수 있던 개수만큼의 결계 제어봉을 추가로 지급해드릴 예정입니다.
지급 완료 이후 본 공지를 통해 추가 안내드리겠습니다.

(예시 : 결계 제어봉을 18개, 또는 19개 보유한 상태에서 해당 오류를 경험하지 않고
정상적으로 결계 제어봉이 2개 또는 1개만 지급된 경우 추가 지급 대상자)



오류로 인해 이벤트 참여에 불편을 드려 죄송합니다.

더욱 안정적인 서비스 제공을 위해 최선을 다하겠습니다.

Princess Nou Revamp



Developer Notes

With the revamp of Hieizan Temple, Princess Nou has also been updated. All prerequisite stages have been removed, allowing you to challenge Boss Princess Nou directly without any prior steps.
Her attack patterns have been enhanced, with improvements to existing mechanics and the addition of new ones. She is now a more powerful yet straightforward boss encounter, offering a refreshing and challenging experience.
Due to the increased difficulty, defeating Princess Nou will now reward you with more Mesos, and you’ll also have a chance to obtain the item “Kanna’s Treasure.”
Alongside this update, the difficulty of Akechi Mitsuhide has also been raised. Face off against these formidable Sengoku Era bosses and enjoy greater rewards for your victories.

  • Princess Nou boss entry method has been changed.
    1. After talking to NPC Ayame, you will automatically be moved to 5F Mail Hall (check spelling) to challenge Princess Nou.
    2. The previous requirement of clearing monsters and the mini-boss from 1F to 4F has been removed.
  • The difficulty of Princess Nou has been increased.
    1. The entry level requirement has been increased from 140 to 180.
    2. The story quest completion requirement has been removed.
    3. Princess Nou's HP has been increased.
  • The boss reward received from defeating Princess Nou has been revamped.
    1. The selling prices of Princess Nou Glowing Soul and Akechi Mitsuhide Glowing Soul have been increased to 25 million Mesos and 33 million Mesos respectively.
    2. The new Kanna's Treasure will become untradeable upon equip, and cannot be equipped at the same time as the original Kanna's Treasure.
    3. Newly added Kanna's Treasure becomes untradeable upon equip and cannot be equipped at the same time as the original Kanna's Treasure.
    4. Platinum Scissors of Karma can be used on the new Kanna's Treasure.
    5. If an original Kanna's Treasure has been destroyed due to failing a Star Force enhancement, it can be transferred onto the new Kanna's Treasure.
  • The set effect of Kanna's Treasure, Hayato's Treasure, and Ayame's Treasure has been removed.
  • The boss mechanics of Princess Nou have been changed.



Princess Nou Story Quest Revamp

Developer Notes

The Hieizan Temple theme dungeon will be receiving a rework. The overall content level has been adjusted to better match player progression, and several story animations and gameplay mechanics have been updated to enhance the experience.
In addition, to make Sengoku Era boss content more accessible, the dungeon will no longer be a prerequisite for challenging Boss: Princess Nou. The exclusive skill tied to this requirement, which is no longer necessary, will also be removed.

  • The Princess Nou story quest level requirement has been increased from level 140 to level 180.
  • EXP obtained from completing the story quest has been changed.
  • Completing the story quest no longer rewards Kanna's Treasure.
  • After completing the story quest, a new medal (The Star of Hieizan) can be obtained.
  • The entry level requirement of some story quest-related maps has been changed to level 180.
  • The difficulty of herb-related quests have been reduced.
  • Exclusive skills obtained from the story quest have been removed.

Union Champion



Developer Notes

Starting July 30th, the Union Champion system will be introduced, offering a new way to elevate your characters to greater heights. This powerful development system allows you to designate a character as a Champion within your world.
As your Champion participates in Champion Trials and climbs the ranks, all Champions will benefit by gaining additional stats. Their progress will contribute to the overall strength of your roster.
Look forward to its Union, where your Champions grow stronger together through the bonds they share!

Requirements
  1. Union Champion will be available on 30th July 2025.
  2. The following requirements must be met to unlock Union Champion.
    • Reached Union Rank of Grand Master Union I or above.
    • At least 1 character at level 260 or above.
    • Completed the quest [Union Champion] Way of the Champion from NPC Ms Appropriation the Dragon Slayer.

Mechanics
  1. Characters at level 260 or above can be registered to a Champion Slot.
    • 2 slots are initially unlocked. When both slots have a character with Champion Rank A, the third slot will be unlocked.
    • A maximum of 3 slots will be unlocked.
    • Only one of each class can be registered to a Champion Slot.
    • Characters registered as Champions cannot perform Free Job Advancement or be deleted.
  2. Successfully completing a Champion Evaluation Match with a registered Champion will advance the Champion Rank of that character.
    • Registered Champions begin at Rank C, and can be advanced to Rank SSS.
    • Champion Evaluation Matches can be accessed through NPC Imperial Knight Sir Hamcelot.
    • After the Match is completed, right click the Champion Slot in the Union Champion UI and select "Rank Up" to apply the new rank.
  3. Stats provided by the Champion Insignia will be enhanced depending on Champion Ranks, and are applied to all Union Champion Characters in the same world.
    • Champion Insignia stats will be applied from Rank B.
    • Union Champion Characters can use Champion's Protection, a powerful but temporary stat buff.
  4. When a Union Champion Character is unregistered from a Champion Slot, a fee of 1 billion Mesos will be incurred. Additionally, any new characters registered to the Champion Slot will have their Champion Rank reset to C.
    • If the third Union Champion slot has already been unlocked, it will not be reset upon unregistering a Union Champion Character.
  5. Union Champion related achievements have been added.

Champion Insignia
  • Champion Insignia's stats will not be applied in boss map until v245.
  • Stats will be applied to Champion Characters in the World according to Champion Rank.
  • Each Champion gets an insignia based on the rank and Champion Insignia is accumulated as the character ranks up.
  • The Champion Insignia obtained by the champions will have the stats added together and applied to all Champion Characters.
  • Insignia stats are as follows:
    1. Rank B: All Stats +20, Max HP/HP +1,000
    2. Rank A: ATT/MATT +10
    3. Rank S: Damage against Boss Monster +5%
    4. Rank SS: Critical Damage +3%
    5. Rank SSS: Ignore DEF +5%



Champion Raid: Dragon Island

Requirements
  1. The following requirement must be met to participate in Champion Raid.
    • At least 2 Level 260+ characters who are registered as Champions.
    • When at least 2 Champions are registered, press the “Champion Battlefield” button in the Union UI while logged in as a Champion Character to participate in the Champion Raid.
  2. Complete [Union Champion] Way of the Champion quest through NPC Ms. Appropriation the Dragon Slayer then register a character as a champion in the Champion tab in the Union UI.
  3. Register at least 2 Champions then press the 'Champion Battlefield' button in the Union UI with your selected champion character to participate in the Champion Raid.

Mechanics - Champion Raid
  1. Click the 'Champion Battlefield' button in the Maple Union UI to participate in Champion Raid and you will be moved to the waiting map 'Wanderer's Hut'.
  2. Talk to NPC Imperial Knight Sir Hamcelot on the left side of the map to open the Champion Raid UI. Click “Start Challenge” to start the Champion Raid.
  3. Upon clicking the button, you will be moved to “Preparation Stage: Path to the Gorge” and can prepare for combat for 5 minutes.
    • When entering the map, all buffs except for time-limited event skills and Champion Double Up Leader are removed.
    • Entering as a party is not allowed. If you are in a party when talking to NPC Imperial Knight Sir Hamcelot, the party will be automatically disbanded.
    • Only the Use tab of the inventory can be accessed, and only the Champion Raid-specific power elixir may be used during Champion Raid.
    • You can talk to NPC Imperial Knight Sir Hamcelot a the right side of the map to enter the Dragon Island battle map.
    • Talk to NPC Imperial Knight Sir Hamcelot on the right side of the map to enter the Dragon Island battle map.
    • When 5 minutes have elapsed while in “Preparation Stage: Path to the Gorge”, you will automatically be moved to the Dragon Island battle map.
  4. Upon entering the Dragon Island, you will begin stage 1 with the selected Champion Character.
  5. Champion Characters can be switched out using the Tab and Number keys.
    • ach champion can be controlled for a maximum of 3 minutes. When 3 minutes is up, that champion can no longer be used.
    • Champions that are not active will automatically charge their “Bond Meter”.
    • When swapping to a champion with a full Bond Meter, they will receive an additional 25% Final Damage.
    • Champion Characters begin with 50% Bond Meter
  6. There are 6 stages in total, each consisting of 10 missions. Missions alternate between defeating normal monsters and boss monsters. Upon finishing a mission, you will gain points
  7. Some monster attacks and stage hazards will inflict a status effect, including “Climate Stacks” on hit. Accumulating 5 “Climate Stacks” will result in a stronger status effect.
    • “Climate Stacks” and other status effects can be cleared by pressing the NPC/Gather Key near fruit-shaped objects placed around the map.
    • The damage over time effect will not reduce the character’s HP to 0 but will not be reset on death.
  8. All 10 missions must be completed before moving onto the next stage.
  9. When the Champion Raid ends, you will be moved to “Dragon Island: Exit Map”.
  10. Talk to NPC Imperial Knight Sir Hamcelot to claim your rewards and move to “Wanderer’s Hut”.
    • 1 coin can be obtained for each point gained.
    • Points gained from Champion Raid are shared among characters in the same World.
    • There are no retry limit but you must score more points that your previous high score to gain champion coins equal to the difference between your new high score and previous high score.
    • If you exit from Dragon Island before claiming your points, no rewards will be given and the stage progress will reset.
  11. Champion Raid progress record is reset on every 1st of the month at 0000hrs.

Mechanics - Champion's Protection
  1. Champion Coins obtained from the Champion Raid can be used to apply the Champion’s Protection buff for 30 minutes.
  2. The Champion’s Protection buff can be found in the Beginner tab of the Skill UI.
    • On double clicking, a UI for customising the buff will appear, and the additional stats you receive from the buff can be customised.
  3. The Champion’s Protection buff effects are as follows. Each individual buff has 5 levels.
    • All Stats/Max HP Increase: +10/+500, +20/+1,000, +30/+1,500, +40/+2,000, +50/+2,500
    • ATT/MATT Increase: +5, +10, +15, +20, +25
    • Damage Against Boss Monster Increase: +2%, +4%, +6%, +8%, +10%
    • Ignore DEF Increase: +2%, +4%, +6%, +8%, +10%
    • Critical Damage Increase: +0.50%, +1.00%, +1.50%, +2.00%, +2.50%
    • Additional EXP Obtained Increase: +5%, +10%, +15%, +20%, +25%
  4. The number of Champion Coins required to activate Champion’s Protection increase with the total strength of Champion’s Protection.
    • The final cost will be the sum of each individual buff added together.
    • The Additional EXP Obtained Increase effect costs 3 times the number of Champion Coins.
  5. The number of Champion Coins required per stat buff per level are as follows:
    • Stat Level 1: 50 Champion coins
    • Stat Level 2: 125 Champion coins
    • Stat Level 3: 200 Champion coins
    • Stat Level 4: 300 Champion coins
    • Stat Level 5: 500 Champion coins

Abyssal Expedition: El Nath Mountains


9th July 2025, (After Maintenance) ~ 19th August 2025, 2359hrs

Developer Notes

The Abyssal Expedition team has made its return to El Nath. Begin your expedition by enlisting mercenaries to battle through waves of monsters and gain experience as they progress.
As your mercenaries grow stronger, you’ll face increasingly powerful bosses. Overcome these challenges to claim the final reward—The Abyssal Expedition Union Block.



Available to Level 200+, Zero character who has completed Chapter 2 for Story Quest



To start of your expedition, click the Maple Expedition button to bring up the UI.
Double click on any of the mercenaries to add them to your team.



Achieve the playtime daily and receive Expedition Supplies.
As you increase your Command Rank, you will unlock more slots for mercenaries and better Expedition Supplies



Once your mercenaries are set, use the portal at the left side of the map to start your journey!



Depending on the level of the map, you can either enter the left white portal for grinding exp or the orange portal in the map for boss.
As your mercenaries get stronger, you unlock maps with stronger monsters and bosses.



Let the mercenaries do the job for you as they will attack the monsters automatically.
Obtain EXP when monsters has been defeated.
You can only stay on the map for a set duration.
As you get stronger, you unlock more maps and stay on the map longer up to 8 hours daily.

    🔹 You can assign up to 3 characters in the selected World and the Challengers World where you created the character.
    🔹 You can designate one of them as the leader first.
    🔹 You can designate the remaining two as followers.

    Double Up Attendance
    🔹 Double Up Attendance check-in can only be done by the leader by hunting monsters within the level range.
    🔹 Each Champion will receive 2,500 Double Up Coins for each completed attendance. A dedicated Coin Shop is available for each Champion character.
     (7,500 total for each attendance)

    Double Up EXP Points Accumulation
    🔹 Double Up EXP Points can only be accumulated by the Leader.
    🔹 Every time the leader hunts a monster within the level range, the followers' Double Up EXP Points will increase. (Up to 100,000 per week)
    🔹 Log in as a follower to spend Double Up EXP Points and EXP.

    Exclusive Leader Benefits (Champion Double Up Leader)
    🔹 Leader will have a special skill located in the beginner tab to summon both Follower.


    🔹 Skill ends after hunting 100,000 level range monsters (-20/+20 Level).
      ↪ EXP Obtained +15%
      ↪ Damage on Normal Monsters +15%



    Note
    🔹 You can do this in the selected World and the Challengers World where you created the character!
    🔹 The designated leader and followers can never be changed to another character.
    🔹 You can only change your leader once a week from among your designated followers.
    🔹 Once understood, select I understand now. Go Assign a leader..

    CHAMPION DOUBLE UP


    IMPORTANT GUIDE

    Setting World
    1. Click Set in the Leader Box.


    2. World Confirmation will be shown. Click Yes to proceed.


    3. Type the (Current World)/Proceed.


    Setting Leader
    1. Click Set in the Leader Box.


    2. Choose the desired leader character and click SELECT.


    3. Click Yes if the desired character selection is final. If not, click No to change.


    4. After confirming the character, type (IGN)/Confirm.


    Setting Follower
    1. Click Set in the Follower Box.


    2. Choose the desired Follower character and click SELECT.


    3. You will be shown a confirmation if you want the selected character to be set as Follower.


    4. After confirming the character, type (IGN)/Proceed.



    IMPORTANT NOTE
     ↪ You cannot change the LEADER and FOLLOWERS after assigning them.
     ↪ You cannot set the same character as both LEADER and FOLLOWERS.
     ↪ You can only change the LEADER from either of the selected FOLLOWERS.
     ↪ Hyper Burning Characters CAN be selected.

    General Progression
  • Eliminate level range monsters (-20/+20 Level) with LEADER to gain points for both Follower.

  • Eliminate level range monsters (-20/+20 Level) with FOLLOWER will not gain any points.
  • Points obtained by both Follower can be converted into EXP points.
  • EXP Points gained will be based on the character's level.

  • Click the USE button to consume 3,000 Maple Points for DOUBLE UP PASS to catch up with the times you missed.
  • Both LEADER and the 2 FOLLOWER will obtain its own Double Up Coins from the DOUBLE UP PASS.

    【职业平衡性调整】

    本次职业平衡性方面主要做了以下调整：统一不同职业的输出循环节奏，并提升爆发输出能力；调整部分职业的生存、辅助能力；统一大部分无法行动技能的冷却时间、再次适用冷却时间以及维持时间，并强化相关显示；除了部分V1技能外，V1新增额外无视防御率效果。详情请各位冒险家前往官网查看《职业平衡性调整公告》。

    为了帮助冒险家更快地达到200级体验五转内容，下调了200级以下星之力战场入场所需星力数值；提高26星以下的强化成功率，并上调强化失败时获得的幸运值；通过上调星之力全区间的幸运值，相较于之前，星之力强化变得更加轻松。

    为了降低冒险家挑战的难度，调整了军团长领主麦格纳斯难度和阿卡伊勒的平衡性，具体调整为：

    麦格纳斯（单人/多人）普通/困难难度，

    【共同】- 冒险家复活后获得2秒无敌效果

    【普通】- 同一颜色的陨石掉落速度已统一调整。

    【普通】- 相同陨石的生成间隔从0上调到1秒（整体冷却时间没有变化）

    【普通】- 回旋斩造成的基于体力百分比的各打击伤害量从15%调整为10%。（共打击3次）

    阿卡伊勒（单人/多人）普通/困难/混沌，在因伦娜的守护内部基于体力百分比受到的伤害量从99%调整为90%，且因伦娜的守护修改为不再过于偏向生成在角落出现。【远古升品石配方变更】
