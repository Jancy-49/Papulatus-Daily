Honorable Heroes! Event


 

As Heroes Fiesta draws to a close, a special event awaits to mark the occasion!

With news of Luminous’s return, Lania has prepared a new event to celebrate the grand finale.

 

■ Event Schedule

September 4, 2025 (After Maintenance) ~ September 17, 2025 23:59 (UTC+0)

 

■ Eligibility

Minted characters who have completed the '[Heroes Fiesta] Celebration for Heroes' Quest

 

■ How to Accept the Event

1. Click ‘[Heroes Fiesta] Honorable Heroes!’ from the Event tab on the left side of your screen.

2. Speak to NPC Lania in the Hall of Heroes event map to begin.

NPC Lania

 

■ How to Participate

After accepting the event, you must complete both of the following conditions each day to qualify for the attendance reward:

1. Obtain the daily maximum of 300 Fiesta Coins.

2. Complete one of the following minigame mission requirements:

  • Win Reverse Battle 2 times
  • Achieve 1st place in Monster Pyramid 2 times
  • Earn 30+ Coins in Code Guessing 2 times
  • Win Reverse Battle 1 time + Achieve 1st place in Monster Pyramid 1 time
  • Win Reverse Battle 1 time + Earn 30+ Coins in Code Guessing 1 time
  • Achieve 1st place in Monster Pyramid 1 time + Earn 30+ Coins in Code Guessing 1 time

 

Once both conditions are fulfilled, click the [Claim] button in the event popup to receive your reward.

Complete all 10 days of attendance to collect a total of 5,000 Fiesta Coins, 30,000 Honor EXP, and the ‘Honorable Heroes’ Title with its unique buff effect.
