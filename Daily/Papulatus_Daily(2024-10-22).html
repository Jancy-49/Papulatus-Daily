10/24(목) 넥슨 정기점검 안내


你好, 넥슨 고객 여러분.

매주 목요일은 넥슨 정기점검으로 10월 24일(목) 오전 6시부터 오전 9시까지
직접 결제 서비스 이용이 원활하지 않습니다.

 
고객 여러분들의 너그러운 양해 부탁드리며
자세한 점검시간과 작업영향은 아래 내용을 확인해 주시기 바랍니다.

▣ 점검시간과 작업영향

- 오전 6시 ~ 오전 9시(3시간)
: 직접 결제 서비스 이용이 원활하지 않습니다.
: 게임 코인 서비스 이용이 원활하지 않습니다.

- 오전 7시 ~ 오전 7시 30분(30분)
: 상점 이용이 원활하지 않습니다.

안내 드리는 내용 참고하셔서 이용에 불편 없으시길 바랍니다.

谢谢.

[Updated] October 2024 Community Contests and Events


The MapleStory Community Team runs a number of contests and events throughout the year, challenging the in-game skill and real-world creativity of Maplers all around the world! Take a look at the new and upcoming Community Contests and Events below, and be prepared to test your skills!

 

TABLE OF CONTENTS

New Contests

  • Halloween Glow Up!
  • Nightmare on Maple Street
  • Main Character Make Over!

 

 

NEW CONTESTS

Halloween Glow Up!

Contest Duration:

  • PDT/PST (UTC -7/-8): Monday, October 21st, 2024 11:00 AM - Monday, November 11th, 2024 11:00 AM
  • CEST/CET (UTC +2/+1): Monday, October 21st, 2024 8:00 PM - Monday, November 11th, 2024 8:00 PM
  • AEDT (UTC +11): Tuesday, October 22nd, 2024 5:00 AM - Tuesday, November 12th, 2024 6:00 AM

Summer is officially out of the picture and it's time to settle into the spooky hours with Halloween Glow Up! Send us a before and after picture of your character in any of our Halloween style accessories/outfits for your chance to earn some rewards! 

How to Enter:

  • Participate by posting in the 'Halloween Glow Up Contest' thread within the official-contests forum on the Official MapleStory Discord and include the following with your entry:
    • In-game name
    • World name
    • Screenshot of your character BEFORE and AFTER you changed your outfit.

Requirements & Rules:

  • One entry per account
  • Image must be safe for work.

The Community Managers and VMs will select 3 best entries for winners and 3 other random participants to win rewards.

Event Prizes:

  • [Updated 10/21] 1st Place will receive:
    • Observatory Chair

    • Symbol Selector Coupon
    • Onyx Apple x10
    • 2x EXP Coupon(15 min) x5
  • 2nd - 3rd Place will receive:
    • Mochi Ice Cream Chair
    • Onyx Apple x10
    • 2x EXP Coupon(15 min) x5
  • 3 Random Participants will receive:
    • Onyx Apple x10
    • 2x EXP Coupon(15 min) x5

Submissions will close on November 11th at 11:00 AM PST!

 

Nightmare on Maple Street

Contest Duration:

  • PDT/PST (UTC -7/-8): Monday, October 21st, 2024 11:00 AM - Friday, November 15th, 2024 11:00 AM
  • CEST/CET (UTC +2/+1): Monday, October 21st, 2024 8:00 PM - Friday, November 15th, 2024 8:00 PM
  • AEDT (UTC +11): Tuesday, October 22nd, 2024 5:00 AM - Saturday, November 16th, 2024 6:00 AM

Darkness is creeping into Maple World as Halloween draws near. It’s time to transform your cozy homes into haunted havens. Let your imagination run wild as you decorate with ghoulish glee. Join our Nightmare on Maple Street event for your chance to earn these terrifyingly great rewards! 

How to Enter:

  • Participate by posting in the 'Nightmare on Maple Street Contest' thread within the official-contests forum on the Official MapleStory Discord and include the following with your entry:
    • In-game name
    • World name
    • Upload a screenshot of your Halloween Themed home!

Requirements & Rules:

  • One entry per account
  • Image must be safe for work.
  • Artwork must be your own

The Community Managers and VMs will select 3 best entries for winners and 3 other random participants to win rewards.

Event Prizes:

  • 1st Place will receive:
    • Choose 1: (Coloring Book Chair or Fairytale Castle Chair)

      • Onyx Apple x10
      • 2x EXP Coupon(15 min) x5
    • 2nd - 3rd Place will receive:
      • Bubble Gum Damage Skin
      • Onyx Apple x10
      • 2x EXP Coupon(15 min) x5
    • 3 Random Participants will receive:
      • Onyx Apple x10
      • 2x EXP Coupon(15 min) x5

Submissions will close on November 15th at 11:00 AM PST!

 

Main Character Make Over!

Contest Duration:

    • PDT/PST (UTC -7/-8): Wednesday, October 23rd, 2024 11:00 AM - Saturday, November 23rd, 2024 11:00 AM
    • CEST/CET (UTC +2/+1): Wednesday, October 23rd, 2024 8:00 PM - Saturday, November 23rd, 2024 8:00 PM
    • AEDT (UTC +11): Thursday, October 24th, 2024 5:00 AM - Sunday, November 24th, 2024 6:00 AM

Calling all spooky-savvy Maplers! Get ready to unleash your inner artist and show off your Halloween spirit. Draw your main MapleStory character in a frightfully fabulous costume and enter our art contest for a chance to win awesome rewards.

How to Enter:

    • Participate by posting in the 'Main Character Make Over Contest' thread within the official-contests forum on the Official MapleStory Discord and include the following with your entry:
      • In-game name
      • World name
      • Upload your drawing of your MapleStory main account character/class in any Halloween themed costume. (Witch, Cat, Ogre, etc)

Requirements & Rules:

    • Only one entry per account.
    • Image must be safe for work.
    • Artwork must be your own.
    • You are able to delete and re-enter your entry if needed. 

The Community Managers and VMs will select the 3 best entries for winners and 5 other random participants to win rewards.

Event Prizes:

    • 1st Place will receive:
      • Toasted Taiyaki Mount

      • 5K Maple Point Coupon
      • Onyx Apple x10
      • 2x EXP Coupon(15 min) x5
    • 2nd - 3rd Place will receive:
      • Panda Mount
      • 2k Maple Reward Points
      • Onyx Apple x10
      • 2x EXP Coupon(15 min) x5
    • 5 Random Participants:
      • Onyx Apple x10
      • 2x EXP Coupon(15 min) x5

Submissions will close on November 23rd at 11:00 AM PST!
