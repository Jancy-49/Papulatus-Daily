    Miracle Summer - June 28 - Tops/Bottoms/Outfits/Capes


    Miracle Summer is back for another year! Throughout the summer, Miracle Time will be enabled for equipment for a specific slot, giving you double the chance to Rank-Up your gear once! Each slot will have a Miracle Time window, so keep your eyes peeled for news on upcoming Miracle Time announcements!

    This Saturday, June 28, 2025, Maplers on all Worlds will have Miracle Time enabled for Tops, Bottoms, Outfits, and Cape Equipment! Cube your shirts, breastplates, and blouses, your shorts, skirts, and greaves, your robes, dresses, and trenchcoats, and even your cloaks, backpacks, and capes of all sorts, all-day Saturday with the improved Rank-Up chances!

    Note: Miracle Summer dates are in UTC, precise times may differ based on your time zone.

    Miracle Time - Tops/Bottoms/Outfits/Capes

    Event Duration:

    • PDT (UTC -7): Friday, June 27, 2025 5:00 PM - Saturday, June 28, 2025 4:59 PM
    • CEST (UTC +2): Saturday, June 28, 2025 2:00 AM - Sunday, June 29, 2025 1:59 AM
    • AEST (UTC +10): Saturday, June 28, 2025 10:00 AM - Sunday, June 29, 2025 9:59 AM

    Event Details:

    • During the noted periods, you will be able to Cube away on Equipment for the Top (including Outfit items), Bottom, and Cape slots with Glowing Cubes, Bright Cubes, and Bonus Glowing Cubes, as well as Bonus Bright Cubes if you have them, with double the chance to Rank-Up equipment once when cubing!
    • During the Miracle Time period, using a Cash Cube to upgrade an item to Legendary Rank will activate the "Double Miracle Time Blessing" buff for the map.
    • Equipment for other slots does not benefit from this instance of the Miracle Summer event.

    Upcoming Miracle Summer Events

    • July 26: Gloves
    • August 9: Accessories and more
    • August 23: Shoes
