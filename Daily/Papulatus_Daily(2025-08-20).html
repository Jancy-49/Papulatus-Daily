    Star Force Revamp


    Developer Notes

    Star Force has been reworked to provide a smoother and more rewarding equipment enhancement experience. With this update, the maximum Star Force level has been expanded to 30 stars, allowing players to push their gear to even greater heights of power.
    To balance this expansion and ensure that reaching comparable strength levels feels faster and more accessible, the success rates for enhancements have been adjusted. In addition, Star Force will now no longer downgrade upon enhancement failure, making the process less punishing and more consistent for all players. Alongside these changes, the enhancement attempt cost has been revised to better reflect the true value of your equipment.
    To celebrate this major rework, we’ll also be hosting a special Star Force event, giving everyone the chance to experience the new system at a reduced cost. Don’t miss out on this opportunity to test the power of your upgraded gear!



    • The maximum applicable Star Force Enhancement tier for gear with an equip level cap of level 138 or higher is increased to 30 stars.
    • Items with an existing Star Force Enhancement tier of 23 or more stars before v244 update will have their Star Force Enhancement tier and stats corrected based on the enhancement tier.
      1. 23 Stars -> 25 Stars
      2. 24 Stars -> 26 Stars
      3. 25 Stars -> 27 Stars
    • When enhancing from Star Force 15 Stars and above, failure no longer results in downgrade, and the success/failure probability is changed.
    • Increased the Meso cost for Enhancement at Star Force 17 Stars, 19 Stars, and 21 Stars.
    • The cost of Anti-Destruction is increased from 100% to 200% of the base Meso consumed during Enhancement.
    • Anti-Destruction will now be available on Star Force 17 Stars equipment.
    • Sunday Maple effect for Star Force Enhancements has been updated.
      1. Star Force Enhancements at 21 Stars and below now have 30% less chance to be destroyed.
    • New achievements for Star Force.
      1. [Star Force] 26 stars!
      2. [Star Force] 27 stars!
      3. [Star Force] 28 stars!
      4. [Star Force] 29 stars!
      5. [Star Force] 30 stars!
    • Demon Avenger's conversion Star Force is limited to a maximum of 410 Stars.



    Golden Hammer Revamp

    • From v244 onward, Golden Hammer is no longer obtainable via certain content.
    • The upgrade count of all equipment where Golden Hammer can be used has been increased by 1.
    • Golden Hammer possessed in inventory can be used to obtain Spell Trace.



    Equipment Revamp

    • The required stats for equipping an item have been removed.
    • The Durability system has been removed except for tools used on gathering/mining.
    • Bow, Crossbow and Ancient Bow no longer knockback enemies with a certain probability.
    • Skill level increase effect has been removed in certain set effect.
    • Certain item explanation has been removed.
    • Similar information has been grouped together and added some categories to improve readability.
    • At the top of the tooltip, scroll enhancement, additional options, Potential, Additional Potential enhancement status is now displayed.
    • If Star Force enhancement is 23 stars and above, effect will be added to the top of the tooltip.
    • For currently equipped items, will now display the equipment is currently equipped instead of combat power increase amount.
    • Will display sum of all additional options levels.
    • Will display detailed info on each additional options.
    • Will display level of each additional options.
    • Will show rank of each Potential and Additional Potential.
    • Soul charge information is no longer displayed in tooltip, moved to dedicated Soul Weapon UI.
    • The currently equipped items in a set will now be displayed at the top of the set item list.
    • Set effect tooltip readability improved.
    • Lucky item effect tooltip readability improved.
    • If the tooltip is cut off by the resolution limit, it will now be continued on the right of the screen.
    • The way certain information is displayed in the equipment tooltip is changed.
    • Equipment tooltip background is changed to a darker color.
    • Equipment tooltip no longer displays the trait EXP gained when equipped once.
    • When displaying the tooltip, press the NPC/Gather key to display an increase in the number of improvements under the scroll information.
    • If the equipment does not use Vicious' hammer, display the grey hammer icon and the text "Vicious' Hammer application 0/1".
    • If Vicious' hammer is used for the equipment, display the purple hammer icon and the text "Apply Vicious' Hammer 1/1".
    • When Silver Wolf type weapon is equipped, the Sengoku 3 equipment set count becomes + 1.

    VIP System Introduction

    Developer Notes

    A brand-new VIP system has been introduced to MapleStory, designed to enhance your gameplay experience with exciting new features. With access to VIP-only channels and the Quick Pass, you’ll be able to enjoy smoother adventures and faster progress than ever before.
    In addition, you can now earn VIP Points whenever you purchase Cash items. These points can be redeemed not only for in-game rewards but also for real-world items, offering more value and flexibility with every purchase.
    The VIP system also comes with practical benefits such as discounts on Star Force enhancement costs, as well as daily and weekly Quick Pass options that help streamline your gameplay.
    Finally, don’t miss the chance to explore the VIP-exclusive lounge, where even more special perks and rewards await. Step inside and make the most of your VIP status!



    The VIP System is a system in MapleStory that allows you to receive special benefits based on the amount of cash you spend.

    Various benefits are prepared for VIP members and these benefits become more powerful as your tier increases.



    VIP Points



    • VIP Point is a currency exclusive to the VIP System that can be obtained when purchasing items from the Cash Shop.
    • Your VIP Tier is determined by calculating the VIP Points you have obtained and you can use these points to purchase various items from the VIP Point Shop.
    • Even if you are not a VIP member, you will obtain 10 VIP Points for every 1,000 Cash spent.
    • Ratio of VIP Points obtained by VIP Tier are as follows:
      1. VIP Silver: 10 VIP Points per 1,000 Cash spent.
      2. VIP Gold: 20 VIP Points per 1,000 Cash spent.
      3. VIP Diamond: 30 VIP Points per 1,000 Cash spent.
      4. VIP Royal: 60 VIP Points per 1,000 Cash spent.
      5. VIP Royal Black: 60 VIP Points per 1,000 Cash spent.
    • VIP Points obtained will expire at midnight on 31st December of the following year.
    • When purchasing cash items through Gift function, you will not be able to obtain VIP Points. (Updated 20 Aug, 1806hrs)



    VIP Tier

    • Tier achievement is calculated in real time based on VIP Points. You will Tier Up to the next Tier once you have obtained enough VIP Points.
    • When you achieve a new tier, the tier retention condition VIP Points will be reset, and you will need to obtain VIP Points during the new tier retention period in order to retain your tier.
    • VIP Points required to retain your VIP Tier is calculated based on the VIP Points obtained over the past 100 days and the tier is reflected at midnight.
    • If the required VIP Points are not obtained during the VIP Tier retention period, the tier will be lowered by one.
    • If the tier is lowered, you must obtain the required VIP Points for the next tier from that moment onward to achieve the next tier.

    Do Gong's Magic Training


    20th August 2025, (after v244 Patch) ~ 30th September 2025, 2359hrs



    Available to Level 200+ with 5th job advancement completed, Zero character who has completed Chapter 2 for Story Quest
    Event Requirements: Junior Designer Grade and above
    Look for the [Night Troupe] Do Gong's Magic Training quest from NPC Do Gong to begin.




    Simple Guide
  • Designate a Training character and a Focus Target character.
  • Eliminate level range monsters (-20/+20 Level) with Training character, a portion of the EXP it earns is automatically shared with the Focus Target character.
  • Login to the Focus Target character to claim the EXP.
  • Characters that has participated in on going Champion Burning event can take advantage of this event as well.

    In depth details
  • You may only assign your Training and Focus Target characters once per World. Once it's done, it can't be changed or canceled by any means. So choose carefully.
  • A character whose Level is higher than the Training Character's can't be assigned as the Focus Target. Even after assignment, if the Focus Target's Level surpasses the Training Character's, EXP sharing will stop.
  • Only characters between Level 200 and below 260 can be assigned as the Focus Target. Naturally, once the Focus Target Level 260, EXP sharing will immediately stop, so it's best to assign a lower-level character if possible.
  • EXP is earned through the Secret Magic training. After assigning both a Training Character and Focus Target and learning the Secret Magic, a special Do Gong's Secret Magic UI will appear on the Training Character's screen. The UI shows you the progress of your Focus Target's growth.
  • For the displayed EXP to actually be transferred, you must log in with the Focus Target character. If you don't log in to the Focus Target and collect any remaining EXP before the event ends, the EXP will disappear.
  • Also while you're logged into your Focus Target to distribute Secret Magic EXP, you can't gain more EXP or level up. Also, certain features like map movement and quest start will be restricted. It's best to wait until the transfer is completed before continuing to play. EXP also can't be transferred on the World Alliance channel or Crossworld Maps.
  • EXP distribution rate based on the Focus Target's Level.
      ➢ Lv. 200 - 210: 150%
      ➢ Lv. 211 - 220: 300%
      ➢ Lv. 221 - 230: 600%
      ➢ Lv. 231 - 240: 900%
      ➢ Lv. 241 - 250: 1,200%
      ➢ Lv. 250 - 259: 1,500%
  • Note that the EXP being distributed is calculated only from base EXP, excluding bonuses from buffs and special effects.
  • Characters participating in Do Gong's Magic Training can't World Leap or change their name.
