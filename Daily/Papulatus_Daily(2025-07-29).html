    Account Restrictions Due to Abuse Detection


    29 Jul 2025

    Account Restrictions Due to Abuse Detection



    Dear Maplers,

    We would like to inform you that temporary blocks have been applied to accounts found involved in abnormal item behavior through the use of unauthorized enhancement methods or services, to allow further investigation to be conducted.

    Actions:
    - Affected accounts have been temporarily restricted.
    - Penalties applied may range from itemized rollback to the complete removal of the associated equipment, depending on the severity and extent of abuse detected.
    - In severe cases, accounts found to be intentionally abusing the system may face permanent bans.

    Reminder:
    - Drop-trading items as a way to bypass intended systems is also considered an inappropriate practice and may result in further action.
    - Offering or receiving item enhancement services (e.g. cubing, scrolling, or star-forcing done by another player) is not recognized as a legitimate gameplay method.
    - Real Money Trading (RMT), which refers to the buying or selling of in-game items, currency, or services using real-world money, is prohibited and will result in strict actions, including permanent suspension.

    We’d like to remind everyone that this was clearly stated in our previous announcement and continues to be enforced.
    If you come across any activity that seems suspicious, we encourage you to report it directly through our iBox or Live Chat support portals.

    Let’s continue to build a fair Mapling environment together.

    – MapleStorySEA Administrator
