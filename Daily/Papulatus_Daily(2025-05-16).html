Steam Community Market (BETA) Closure Notice


Hello Maplers,

We want to sincerely thank you for the incredible support and enthusiasm you’ve shown for the Steam Community Market (BETA), launched in celebration of the 20th Anniversary of Global MapleStory.

We truly appreciate all the feedback we've received and are excited to explore new ideas and initiatives inspired by your input. The Steam Community Market (BETA) feature will be coming to an end and will be disabled in the game. Please refer to the following details for more information.

We will be officially closing the Steam Community Market (BETA) feature on August 27, 2025 at 12:00 AM UTC.

Effective immediately after the maintenance on May 15, 2025:

  • Oswell’s Scissors of Karma cannot be used.
  • Oswell’s Scissors of Karma cannot be purchased in the Cash Shop.
  • Items cannot be registered in the Steam Inventory.

We will share more detailed information about the closure and refund processes in a separate notice.

Thank you again for your support and feedback. Our priority will always be to deliver a stable and enjoyable gameplay experience for our players.

Thank you for your patience and understanding.

- The MapleStory Team

Changes and Updates:

  • MapleStory will be updated to v.259.3.0.
  • Resolved an issue where players were not able to obtain rewards from Xiao Xiao's Level-up Support on a newly created Mo Xuan character if earlier reward tiers were claimed previously on a separate and deleted Mo Xuan character.
  • Resolved an issue causing players to circumvent chat bans by using the Fast Character Switch feature.
  • Resolved an issue where Xiao Xiao's Level-up Support displayed Arcane Symbol Selector Coupons as a reward for reaching Lv. 200.
  • Resolved an issue where Divine Art: Crashing Earth's Cooldown would be reduced only when Heir of the Divine's Reincarnation feature was inactive and not while it was active.
  • Resolved an issue where the Lynn job would appear as Jianghu when viewed in the Character Select screen and the Switch Character UI.
  • Resolved a text issue where the Victoria Cup Completion Box would incorrectly show granting a Lovely Sugar Set Selector Coupon instead of a Mushroom Garden Set Selector Coupon.
  • Resolved an issue where other Mo Xuan characters are visible during a cutscene.
  • Resolved a text issue where the 'Ultimate Collab' buff would list the incorrect names from the event.
  • Resolved a text issue where Arwen is labeled as Lania within the Ultimate Collab UI and description.
  • Resolved an issue where the Magnificent Growth Potion did not indicate the correct amount players would receive for the 19th check-in within the Victoria Cup Stamp Book UI.
  • Resolved an issue where Mo Xuan's Secondary Weapon would show in a blank tab when trying to purchase one from NPC Neville.
  • [Updated 5/14] Resolved an issue where players would receive a notification from Lord Balrog when defeating an Elite Mob.
  • [Updated 5/14] Resolved a text issue where the '[Long-distance Race] Cassandra's Suggestion' quest would list the incorrect event period.
  • [Updated 5/14] Reduced number of laps needed to complete the race in the [Victoria Cup] Boat Racing mini-game from 3 to 2.
    • Please note that it will still display the number of laps needed as 3 but only require 2 laps to be completed.
  • [Updated 5/15] Resolved an issue where players were able to check-in multiple times within a day for the Collection Book in the [Victoria Cup] Homecoming Mission event.
