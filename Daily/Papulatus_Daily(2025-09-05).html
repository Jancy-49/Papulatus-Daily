[Completed] Scheduled Minor Patch - September 4, 2025


[Updated 11:40 AM PDT] The maintenance has been completed and we have extended Cash Shop items in your inventory for 7 hours. Please restart your Nexon Launcher or Steam to make sure that you get the latest client! Thank you for your patience.

We will be performing a scheduled minor patch starting on Thursday, September 4, 2025 at 5:00 AM PDT (8:00 AM EDT / 2:00 PM CEST / 10:00 PM AEST). We anticipate the maintenance to last approximately 7 hours, concluding around 12:00 PM PDT (3:00 PM EDT / 9:00 PM CEST / 5:00 AM AEST September 5).

Times:

Thursday, September 4, 2025
PDT (UTC -7): 5:00 AM - 12:00 PM
EDT (UTC -4): 8:00 AM - 3:00 PM
CEST (UTC +2): 2:00 PM - 9:00 PM
AEST (UTC +10): 10:00 PM - 5:00 AM September 5

What will be unavailable:

  • All MapleStory game servers.

Changes and Updates:

  • MapleStory will be updated to v.262.2.0.
  • Monthly Windows Update.
  • Resolved an issue with the portal in the Small Forest Trail during the 'Returning the Empty Lunch Box' Evan quest.
  • Resolved an issue in Challenger World where the Tera Burn character limit would reset after leaping a character that used a Tera Burninator.
  • Resolved an issue where Sia characters are unable to start the '[Legion Champion] Champion's Path' quest.
  • Resolved an issue where the Save the Diver Event was displaying items that are unobtainable from the current event.
  • Resolved an issue where Shadower's skill Shadow Edge was not functioning correctly.
  • Resolved an issue where Korean text would appear multiple times when defeating enemies and collecting Troupe Coins.
  • Resolved an issue where the 'To Spouse' and 'Familiar' key binds would not properly save when being set as a Custom Key Binding.
  • [Updated 9/4/25] Changing of Guild and Alliance names has been reenabled for those affected due to having duplicate Guild or Alliance names after the World Merge.
  • [Updated 9/4/25] Resolved an issue where characters affected by the World Merge were unable to receive the World Merge Commemorative Title.
  • [Updated 9/4/25] Resolved an issue causing Sia Characters Erda Link to reset when leaping from Challenger Worlds.
    • Sia Astelle will now be available to World Leap again.
    • Sia characters that have World Leaped already will have their Erda Link progress restored to when the character was in the Challenger-World.
    • Any resources used in the world you leaped to for upgrading your Erda Link will also be refunded, but this will happen at a later time as we need to gather more information during our investigation.
  • [Updated 9/4/25] Resolved an issue causing the Monster Collection Data to reset after the World Merge.
  • [Updated 9/4/25] Resolved an issue where Meso Market transactions that were started prior to the World Merge were unable to be cancelled after the World Merge.
  • [Updated 9/4/25] Resolved an issue where Sia characters were unable to access the '[Genesis Weapon] Weapon Reissue' quest.
  • [Updated 9/4/25] Resolved an issue where Sia characters would not receive their Secondary during their intro quests.
  • [Updated 9/4/25] Resolved an issue where the Character Switch UI would show invisible and selectable character slots despite having filled the 58 character slots after the World Merge.

Unscheduled Server Maintenance - September 5, 2025

September 5 Temporary Maintenance and NXPC Warp service anomaly.


 

We would like to inform our Maplers of a temporary maintenance affecting the access to the MapleStory Universe Services.

 

▶ Maintenance Schedule

MapleStory Universe Services: September 5, 2025, 15:18 ~ September 6, 2025, 03:30 16:42 (UTC +0)

 

 

▶ Maintenance Area

  • Swap & Warp

 

▶Maintenance Details

  • An issue has been identified where NXPC cannot be received when warping to the AVAX-C Chain or Henesys. 
    • The warped amount is not lost. We are currently investigating the issue to ensure there is no loss or harm to our players, and will provide further updates through an additional notice.
    • The warp function will remain disabled until the issue is fully resolved, and cannot be used during this period.

 

※ Please be advised that the schedule may change in the future.

※ Access to the services will not be available during the maintenance hours.
