Channel Check on 2nd December 2024 (Aquila World)


Dear Maplers,

To facilitate server upkeep works, there will be a scheduled Channel Check for Aquila World on 2nd December 2024, between 0900hrs to 1230hrs (GMT +8).

Specified channels listed above will NOT be available during the respective timings. We seek all Maplers understanding and cooperation, and are advised to move to the unaffected channels to avoid any disruption to gameplay.

During the process, please pay attention to the sliding notice at the top of the game for prompts on the process of the channel check, as well as the channels currently inaccessible.

The schedule for the downtime will be as follows:

World Channels Downtime (GMT+8)
Aquila 3 to 6, 13 to 16, 23 to 26 0900hrs to 1030hrs
Aquila 7 to 10, 17 to 20, 27 to 30 1100hrs to 1230hrs

We apologize for any inconvenience caused.

-MapleSEA Administrator

MapleStory Universe: Gear Up NOW!

MapleStory Universe: Gear Up NOW! Announcement

A heartfelt thank you to everyone exploring MapleStory Universe! Since day one, your insights and feedback have been instrumental in shaping the game, and we deeply value your continued support.

As a way to show our gratitude for the passionate involvement, we are excited to announce that we are now entering the MapleStory Universe: Gear Up period. The first step would be the “Gear Up NOW!” phase that will start after the scheduled maintenance (November 29, 2024 09:00 AM (UTC+0)) ends. Gear Up NOW! will continue until December 23, 2024 00:00 AM (UTC+0). All your In-game data from 2nd Pioneer Test will be continued for the Gear Up NOW! phase.

MapleStory Universe: Gear Up NOW! phase is a key step toward the full-scale live service. Special benefits are being prepared for current participants of the 2nd Pioneer Test, recognizing your passion and dedication. Additionally, new events with valuable in-game rewards will be introduced throughout the Gear Up period. Incredible rewards are prepared for all participants — and what’s more exciting part is: The rewards will be carried over to the official launch.

Gear Up NOW! aims to establish a solid foundation for the service based on your feedback, ensuring a more refined and engaging experience when the official launch arrives.

Please stay tuned for more updates.

Thank you.
