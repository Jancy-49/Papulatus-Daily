What's Your MapleStory? - Krate


MapleStory is a constantly evolving game, full of heroes, villains, and everything in between. But none of them are as important to us as you, our players who make Maple World what it is! The history of MapleStory is a long and winding one, but now we'd like to turn the attention to you, and hear more about your own journeys that you've taken as MapleStory has grown over the years. The question we'd like to ask you all is: What's Your MapleStory? If you'd like to submit your story, you can do so via this form.

For today's edition of What's Your MapleStory, we spoke to Krate about their journey through MapleStory!

How long have you been playing MapleStory?

Hello everyone, my IGN as you can see is Krate, and I got it from the DJ group "Keys N Krates" LOL. I've been playing MapleStory since the pre-Big Bang update, around 2007-08. At this time, I remember I had to delete my character because I had this quest from Chief Stan to deliver a message all the way to Omega Sector, but since my character was low leveled at the time, my character couldn't climb the tall tower up to Ludibrium, so I had to delete my character and start over... I was still in South Korea at the time, so I believe I started playing GMS around 2010-11.

Who's your favorite class, and why?

My current main is a Luminous, but my personal favorite class would be Phantom. The concept of a character throwing cards and stealing skills from other jobs looked very fascinating, which was why Phantom was my main back then when I used to play Interactive servers.

What is your favorite memory while playing MapleStory?

If I had to choose some memories from playing MapleStory, one of them would be using Dark Sight as a low-level thief and exploring high-level areas back then when I was a kid. The BGM was different, and the monsters looked really scary. I remember getting the adrenaline rush even though it was just MapleStory LOL. Another one would be eye shopping through the Free Market back in the day in Interactive servers. I loved seeing these crazy items on the market for a capped meso price, thinking, "What if...?" LOL.

One other memory would be me giving up on one of my permanent friendship rings to move to a different world when the first world leap event was here in GMS. I believe the friend I shared the ring with at the time had already quit the game, so I ended up leaving the world we were in together, but later on, I got a message saying, "Oh, you left me with the ring." HAHAHA. Funny enough, we're still friends outside of the game and still talk to each other.

Shout out to my guild delight r>delight :) LOL

NA Interactive World Merge Is Coming This August 27!


Hey Maplers,

We are excited to announce the NA Interactive World Merge that was mentioned in one of Inkwell’s previous notes. Our goal with this merge is to create a more vibrant and connected experience by encouraging more opportunities for player interaction.

We also want to thank everyone who participated in the world merge survey from March 19th to April 1st. While the majority of the players supported some form of a merge, opinions were split on the number of worlds. After careful review, we’ve decided to move forward with retaining the very first two original worlds, Scania and Bera. We believe this will help create a more balanced population, boost the in-game economy, and improve player interaction, all while keeping our servers stable.

For Maplers whose worlds are affected by this merge, please refer to the details below:

Schedule

  • August 27, 2025 (after maintenance)

Target Worlds

  • Interactive worlds, Aurora and Elysium, will be consolidated into Scania.
  • Scania channels will be increased to a total of 30 channels.

World Merge FAQ

To help you better prepare for the World Merge, please review the following FAQs during this process. Please note that we will be sharing additional updates and details at a later date.

Does this change how I log in?

  • Yes. If you were in Aurora or Elysium, you will now log into your new merged world, Scania, from the world selection screen.

What happens when I exceed the max number of 58 character slots?

  • All character slots from the merging worlds (Scania, Aurora, and Elysium) will be combined, including default slots. Your character display order will be reset.
  • Up to 58 characters will be visible on the character selection screen.
    • If you have more than 58 characters, a Character Selection UI will appear where you must select 58 characters to display. Non-selected characters are not permanently removed but will be temporarily inaccessible until you remove some other characters. This UI will be available until January 2026.
    • If 58 characters are not selected before the UI is removed, the highest level characters will automatically be displayed.
    • You will not be able to create new characters unless the excess characters have been deleted.

What happens to shared features like Storage, Cash Shop and Auction House?

  • Storage Items
    • Storage slots will be merged up to a maximum of 152 slots. Items beyond the limit will be hidden and can be retrieved by removing existing items.
  • Storage Mesos
    • Mesos from all merging worlds will be combined up to 499 billion (499,999,999,999) mesos. Excess mesos will be sent as coins via the Gift Drop.
  • Cash Inventory
    • Items beyond the limit will be hidden and will appear as space is available.
  • Auction House
    • Items beyond the limit will be hidden until space is available. To buy or sell again, you must move items to your character's inventory.

Will my Guild be affected?

  • Guild names must be unique per world. If there are duplicates, names will be automatically renamed with random characters.
  • Renamed guilds and alliances will receive a name change UI upon login, and only the Leader can change the name.
    • If your leader is inactive, please contact Player Support.

Will the Maple Rewards Shop purchase limit reset?

  • No, the purchase limit will not reset with the merge.
  • The next reset will occur with the start of the next calendar month.

We hope these changes allow players in the newly merged worlds to participate in more community activities. We’ll be updating this post as we receive more information. For live updates, join our official Discord server here!

- The MapleStory Team
