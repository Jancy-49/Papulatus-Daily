    Regular Coloring Prism

    The Regular Coloring Prism changes the colors of certain Surprise Style Box items at random, giving you an entirely new look to the outfit! Please note that the item's appearance is immediately changed and cannot be reverted without use of another coloring prism.

    • Price: 5 Star Stamps
      • See the Star Stamp Shop Season 1 section for more details on these new stamps.
    • Price: 5 Style Box Stamps
    • Duration: 30 Days (Coupon), 90 Days (resulting item)
    • Even if used multiple times, the colors will be changed based on the item's original appearance.
    • Special effects and certain weapons will retain their original color even if a Coloring Prism is used.
    • An item must have been equipped or been otherwise made untradeable for a Coloring Prism to be used on it.
    • Outfit items that can be recolored with Coloring Prisms are marked with the Starlight Badge in the item icon, indicating that it has absorbed the power of starlight and can be dyed.:

    Premium Coloring Prism

    The Premium Coloring Prism allows you to select the exact tone, chroma, and brightness you desire of certain Surprise Style Box items!

    • Available within the Mythic Star Style Box
    • See the Mythic Star Style Box section for more details on this new item.
    • Duration: 90 Days
    • Tone, chroma, and brightness can be adjusted via the sliders or by inputting specific values into the fields next to the slider.
    • Even if used multiple times, the changeable colors will be based on the item's original appearance.
    • If you select a color scale choice that doesn't exist for an item, then the color will not change, even if you adjust the tone, chroma, and brightness.
    • Special effects and certain weapons will retain their original color even if a Coloring Prism is used.
    • An item must have been equipped or been otherwise made untradeable for a Coloring Prism to be used on it.
    • Outfit items that can be recolored with Coloring Prisms are marked with the Starlight Badge in the item icon, indicating that it has absorbed the power of starlight and can be dyed:

    Prism Color Restore

    The Prism Color Restore item immediately reverts any color changes done with either the Regular or Premium Coloring Prism, returning the item to its original color! It can be obtained through the Star Stamp Shop and Style Box Stamp Shop.

    • Price: 5 Star Stamps
      • See the Star Stamp Shop Season 1 section for more details on these new stamps.
    • Price: 5 Style Box Stamps
    • Duration: 30 Days (Coupon), 90 Days (resulting item)

    MapleStory N Fair Gameplay Guideline


     

    In MapleStory N, abnormal gameplay is being closely monitored and regulated.

    To ensure fair gameplay, we would like to introduce the play methods recommended by MapleStory N.

    ※ Measures against abnormal gameplay will be continuously strengthened, and please note that failure to follow the guidelines below may result in restrictions on your gameplay.

     

    1. Do not run or use hacking tools during gameplay.

    Do not run cheat engine that can tamper with game data, or programs that modify memory data.

    Running such programs may result in strong actions being taken in accordance with the operation policy.

     

    2. To maintain a fair gameplay environment, do not use macro programs or external input devices that provide functions similar to macros.

    Recently released gamepads and joysticks often come with various built-in macro functions and features that allow easy key input. These have been found to exhibit patterns similar to macro bots, and may result in gameplay restrictions.

    As stated in the game guide and FAQ in Support, using any external input device other than a keyboard may result in service restrictions.

     

    3. Gameplay should only be conducted on the local PC and should not be accessed remotely from another device.

    A significant number of abnormal Maplers access the game from non-local environments (e.g., virtual environments), and active measures are being taken against such Maplers.

    Maplers are advised to take note of this and use the game only on the PC where it is installed.

    Accessing and playing the game from a virtual environment may result in actions being taken in accordance with the operational policy.

     

    4. It is recommended to run only MapleStory N for gameplay.

    Running abnormal programs alongside MapleStory N may prevent proper game activity from being recorded and could be considered an attempt to bypass monitoring, which may interfere with accurate enforcement.

    Such behavior may be referenced when determining the use of illegal programs, so it is advised to run MapleStory N exclusively during gameplay.

     

    5. When the Lie Detector or Violeta system is triggered, please focus on deactivating it as quickly as possible.

    These systems may activate to verify whether MapleStory N is being played by a normal player.

    If activated, gameplay should be paused to concentrate on resolving the detection.

     

    Efforts will be made to provide a healthy gaming environment.

     

    ⚠️ Important Note 

    ※ If a restriction is applied due to a violation of the operational policy, no assistance such as lifting the restriction will be provided.

    ※ Details about gameplay activities that are discouraged in MapleStory N can be found in [Discouraged Gameplay].

    ※ This information was written as of August 2025, and may be subject to change based on future updates.
