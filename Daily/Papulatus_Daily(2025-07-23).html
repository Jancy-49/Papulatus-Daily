    Ride or Die

    Quick Overview

    • Take on up to 5 bosses each week by choosing your difficulty level and setting challenges.
    • Requirement: Lv. 200 and above (Zero characters that completed Story Quest Act 2).
    • The higher the difficulty and number of challenges, the greater the rewards will be and a higher final score you'll receive!
    • Earn rewards like Inspector Coins, Kazax’s Energy Fragments, and weekly Inspector Supply Boxes based on your final score.

    How to Participate

    • Select ‘Ride or Die’ from the Event List and click ‘Participate’ to start the Ride or Die event.
    • Upon accepting the quest, you’ll be teleported to the event map, where you’ll meet Libae, a new inspector who urgently needs your help to save Maple World!

    Setting Boss Difficulty and Challenges

    • Face off against up to 5 bosses per week by choosing your difficulty and how many challenges you want to take on. The 5 bosses you can challenge are:
      • Papulatus
      • Vellum
      • Horntail
      • Darknell
      • Magnus
    • There are four difficulties to choose from:
      • Code B: Easy
      • Code A: Normal
      • Code S: Hard
      • Code X: Extreme
        • Code X Requirements: Only your weapon, secondary weapon, and emblem can be equipped for this challenge. Swapping equipment will be disabled during battle.
        • Test your strength and limits in Code X to earn the highest reward box and a large number of Kazax fragments! With different bosses and fewer challenges this time, Code X is the perfect chance to push for a high score.

    • Ready for a challenge? You can set up to 15 challenges and one will be randomly assigned to you during each run. The following challenges are available:

    • The score multiplier for challenges have been increased, so the more challenges you take on, the higher your weekly score will be!
    • You have a total of 20 lives shared across all 5 bosses per week. If you lose them all, you’re done for the week!
      • Use Practice Mode to familiarize yourself with the bosses! Speak with Libae to adjust the difficulty level and challenges as needed.
        • Practice Mode is unavailable for Code X difficulty.

      • Chosen difficulty

    Althea's Support

    • Althea is here to support you during fights! Once inside the boss map, you’ll receive Althea’s Support, a special skill that charges support up to Lv. 3 as you fight.
      • Charge the skill by collecting support! The more support you receive, the more powerful Althea’s Support skill effect will be!

    • The amount of support required to charge each level is 150.
      • For every 15 support gained, one skill gauge slot fills.
      • Receive a small amount of support at regular intervals.
      • Receive a great amount of support when your character touches a summoned object.
        • The object spawns at a random location every 30 seconds and disappears after a certain amount of time.

    • Once the gauge is full, press the Interact key to activate one of Althea’s support skills at random. These help clear summoned enemies and disrupt Kazax’s interference.
    • Right-click the skill icon to toggle between automatic or manual activation.
