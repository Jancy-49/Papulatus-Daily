[Completed] Unscheduled Minor Patch - January 15, 2025


[Updated 8:26 PM PST] The maintenance has been completed and we have extended Cash Shop items for 3 hours. Please restart your Nexon Launcher or Steam to make sure that you get the latest client! Thank you for your patience.

We will be performing an unscheduled minor patch today, January 15, 2025 at 6:00 PM PST (9:00 PM EST / 3:00 AM CET January 16 / 1:00 PM AEDT January 16). We anticipate the maintenance to last approximately 3 hours, concluding around 9:00 PM PST (12:00 AM EST January 16 / 6:00 AM CET January 16 / 4:00 PM AEDT January 16).

Times:

Wednesday, January 15, 2025
PST (UTC -8): 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM
EST (UTC -5): 9:00 PM - 12:00 AM January 16
CET (UTC +1): 3:00 AM January 16 - 6:00 AM January 16
AEDT (UTC +11): 1:00 PM January 16 - 4:00 PM January 16

  • What will be unavailable:
    • All MapleStory game servers.
  • Changes and updates:
    • MapleStory will be updated to v.256.4.0.
    • Temporarily disable the use of Karma Bright Cube obtained from the Champion Burning Double Up Coin Shop on any equipped item.
      • We are currently investigating this issue, and further details regarding the resolution for players affected by the use of Karma Bright Cubes from the Champion Burning Double Up Coin Shop will be announced in the future. Therefore, we recommend refraining from using additional cubes on the affected equipment items. We apologize for the inconvenience and ask for your understanding.
