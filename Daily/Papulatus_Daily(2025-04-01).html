For Maya Sake


It's time to stop Maya from coughing after 20 years...

2nd April 2025 (After Maintenance) ~ 16th April 2025, 2359hrs



Available to Level 101+, Zero character who has completed Chapter 2 for Story Quest.

How to play
  • Simply hunt 100 Medicine Herb that Can Revive Dead Person from level range monsters (-20/+20 Level) and deliver them to NPC Maya.
  • Every 100 Medicine Herb that Can Revive Dead Person delivered to NPC Maya you will be rewarded 10 Advanced EXP Coupon.
  • There is no daily limit as NPC Maya is really sick for 20 years and counting.
  • During the last day of the event at 16th April 2025, 0000hrs, if the total world Medicine Herb that Can Revive Dead Person count delivered to NPC Maya reaches 999,999, all players that at least delivered 2,020 Medicine Herb that Can Revive Dead Person will receive... 1 Green Mushroom Cap from Maya with gratitude. kek


    Things to note
  • Due to NPC Maya's excessive coughing, her entire house is filled with deadly poison which cause 10% Damage when per second when you enter the Maya's House.
  • You can die from the deadly poison so you have to be quick.
