Get ready to be dazzled by an array of spectacular activities and attractions at Victoria Voyage: Sailing Beyond! From thrilling games to enchanting booths, there's something for everyone to enjoy. Here are the highlights you won’t want to miss:

🍁 Relive the good old days
Take a jog down memory lane as you relive moments from your childhood exploring Victoria Island as we turn 20!

🍁 Exciting Stage Games
Test your skills and compete in exhilarating stage games for a chance to win coveted MapleStorySEA Physical Merchandise and In-game Items. The thrill of victory awaits!

🍁 Dazzling Game Booths
Explore four amazing Game Booths, each one designed with a unique twist to keep you entertained and coming back for more. Familiar favorites and new challenges await you!

🍁 Art Street
Unleash your inner artist at our Art Booths. Showcase your creativity and seize the chance to win mysterious prizes. Your masterpiece could be the key to unlocking incredible rewards!

🍁 Music Performances
Join us on Sunday for a live performance that’s sure to invoke major nostalgia! Close your eyes and allow yourself to be whisked away to simpler times.

🍁 MapleStorySEA Turns 20!!
Join us for cake cutting to celebrate our 20th birthday on Sunday! Expect surprises beyond your wildest imagination as we commemorate this special day with a Fantasy Surprise!

🍁 Lucky Draw Extravaganza
Don’t miss the opportunity to win a thrilling Lucky Draw prizes on the grand finale day. The excitement is electrifying, and the rewards are simply fantastic!

🍁 and so much more...
The wonders of Victoria Voyage don’t end there. Countless surprises and magical moments await you in every corner. Dive deep into the fantasy and let the adventure unfold!

Here's a sneak peek at some of the exclusive rewards waiting for you!

    Have a question? Check here to see if your question is answered! Still have a question? Do contact us via iBox for us to check on it! We will update the FAQ from time to time, so do check back!

    Question: Can I attend the event without purchasing a ticket?
    Answer: Yes! The event is free to attend. Feel free to join us for a trip down memory lane. However, Maplers who RSVP will enjoy a more complete experience.

    Question: Why is there no option to purchase the RSVP Package in MYR?
    Answer: Our tickets can only be purchased in SGD via Eventbrite. This payment method will automatically convert the charge to the currency of your debit or credit card.

    Question: Can I purchase multiple Basic RSVP or Premium RSVP?
    Answer: You are allowed to purchase up to two (2) of each RSVP Pack. The system is actively monitored to prevent abuse.

    Question: I have purchased the RSVP Packs but cannot make it to the event. Can I ask someone to redeem it on my behalf?
    Answer: You may allow someone else to redeem it on your behalf at your discretion. However, we encourage you to redeem your RSVP pack and enjoy the event yourself for the best experience. The Event Organizers are not responsible for any lost tickets in such cases.

    Question: I have received an item from the Stage Events. Can I exchange it for in-game rewards?
    Answer: All rewards from the event are non-exchangeable.

    Question: Is there an expiry for the in-game item codes?
    Answer: All codes related to the Victoria Voyage: Sailing Beyond event can be redeemed until 30th September, 2025, 2359hrs (GMT +8). Please redeem all codes as soon as possible. If you encounter any faulty codes, contact iBox within two weeks of item distribution for prompt support and assistance.

    Question: How many Victoria Voyage Lucky Draw chances can I get if I purchased one RSVP pack?
    Answer: You can receive 1 chance per Basic RSVP Package and 3 per Premium RSVP Package.

    Question: I have won physical merchandise through stage activities. Can I exchange it for in-game rewards or other merchandise?
    Answer: All rewards from the event are non-exchangeable. In case of discrepancies, the event staff's decision will take precedence.

    Question: What if I won the Lucky Draw but was not at the event during the draw time to receive my prize? Will it be forfeited?
    Answer: For the one-of-a-kind physical prizes, you must be present during the Lucky Draw segment at 8:00 PM to receive it. For other prizes, you will be contacted via the registered email address linked to the IGN on your Lucky Draw slip. Ensure you remain contactable via your registered email address. If we are unable to contact you after 30 calendar days, we reserve the right to forfeit your prize and re-draw a winner.

    Question: Is the venue pet-friendly?
    Answer: Pets are not allowed at the venue for safety and hygiene reasons. Unless you have a pet rock, please leave your pets at home.



    Parking:
    Parking Coupons will NOT be provided for the event. Guests are responsible for their own parking fees. For more information, you can check the Sunway Pyramid parking details here.

    Smoking Policy:
    Smoking is strictly prohibited within the event premises.

    Refund and Returns Policy:
    No refunds nor returns will be issued for RSVP Packages and Play Coupons. However, in the unlikely event that an RSVP Package is oversold, a refund will be processed to the card used for payment.

    PDPA (Personal Data Protection Act) Acknowledgment:
    By participating in the Victoria Voyage: Sailing Beyond Event, attendees acknowledge and consent to the collection and use of their personal data for event-related purposes, including, but not limited to, photography and videography. This may involve capturing images for promotional and archival purposes. If you have any concerns about the use of your personal data, please contact our event organizer.

    RSVP Verification:
    Please bring your RSVP E-Ticket for verification to receive your RSVP Pack. Event staff reserves the right to reject invalid tickets.

    Behavior and Conduct:
    All attendees are expected to behave responsibly and respectfully. Any disruptive or inappropriate behavior may result in removal from the event without a refund.

    Lost or Stolen Items:
    The event organizers are not responsible for any lost or stolen items. Please keep your belongings secure.

    Health and Safety:
    Attendees are encouraged to follow all health and safety guidelines provided by event staff. If you feel unwell during the event, please seek assistance immediately from our event staff.

    Event Changes:
    The organizers reserve the right to make changes to the event program, schedule, or venue if necessary. Any significant changes will be communicated to attendees in a timely manner.



  • The information in this post is accurate at the time of writing. However, due to the dynamic nature of the event, the actual experience may vary.
  • In case of any discrepancies, the events and activities at the venue will take precedence.
  • PlayPark Pte Ltd and/or NEXON Korea Corporation reserve the right to amend or adjust the listed content without prior notice.
