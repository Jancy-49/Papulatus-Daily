Miracle Winter - December 28 - Primary Weapons/Secondary Equipment


Miracle Winter has returned to get your gear cubed up! During the winter, Miracle Time will be enabled for equipment for specific slots giving you double the chance to Rank-Up your gear once. Each slot will have a miracle window, so keep your eyes peeled for news on upcoming Miracle Time announcements!

On Saturday, December 28, 2024, Maplers on all Worlds will have Miracle Time enabled for Primary Weapons and Secondary Equipment! Cube all forms of primary weapons, including swords, bows, guns, chakrams, wands, and more, as well as the secondary equipment, such as shields, rosaries, whistles, hex seekers, and more, all-day Saturday with the improved Rank-Up chances!

Note: Miracle Winter dates are in UTC, precise times may differ based on your time zone.

Miracle Time - Primary Weapons/Secondary Equipment

Event Duration:

  • PST (UTC -8): Friday, December 27, 2024 4:00 PM - Saturday, December 28, 2024 3:59 PM
  • CET (UTC +1): Saturday, December 28, 2024 1:00 AM - Sunday, December 29, 2024 12:59 AM
  • AEDT (UTC +11): Saturday, December 28, 2024 11:00 AM - Sunday, December 29, 2024 10:59 AM

Event Details:

  • During the noted periods, you will be able to Cube away on Equipment for Primary and Secondary slots with Glowing Cubes, Bright Cubes, and Bonus Glowing Cubes, as well as Bonus Bright Cubes and Violet Cubes if you have them, with double the chance to rank-up equipment once when cubing!
  • During the Miracle Time period, using a Cash Cube to upgrade an item to Legendary Rank will activate the "Double Miracle Time Blessing" buff for the map.
  • Equipment for other slots does not benefit from this instance of the Miracle Winter event.

Upcoming Miracle Winter Events

  • January 4: Tops, Bottoms, Overalls, and Capes
  • January 11: Shoes
  • January 18: Accessories and more
  • January 25: Gloves
  • February 1: Hats
