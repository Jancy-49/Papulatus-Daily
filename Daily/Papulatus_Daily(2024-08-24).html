Night Troupe Ludibrium


You have received an invitation from Spiegels!

21st August 2024, (after v235 Patch) ~ 17th September 2024, 2359hrs



Developer Notes

The Night Troupe event with a Ludibrium theme is now live! Don’t miss out on the Daily Quest rewards that support up to the Carcion Authentic Symbol, along with the all-new hair movement coupon! Be sure to check out the MapleSEA-exclusive Merlion and Eagle firecracker effects at the Ludibrium Fireworks Festival event.

Available to Level 101+, Zero character who has completed Chapter 2 for Story Quest
Look for the [Night Troupe] Festivals or Dreams, Illusions and Toys quest in the Icon to begin

➢ Designer Grade: Rank UP using Fantasy Coins to get various benefits.
➢ Spiegelmann's Time Travel Ticket: Triggering Event Skills for EXP buff.
➢ Ludibrium Fireworks Festival: Get buffed up from the event map at every xx:30.
➢ Knock Knock Toy Factory: AFK for Fantasy Coins and Traits.
➢ Sneak Attack! Block Papulatus: Hit Toy Papulatus as much as you can to obtain EXP and Fantasy Coins.
➢ Exciting Doll Draw: Obtain EXP Coupons depending what you grabbed.
➢ Run! Bumper bumper car: A 3v3 challenge for level 219 and below - push your opponent out of the map to earn Level rewards.
➢ What if toy blocks fall from the sky?: Use Mesos to provide +50% EXP Buff for everyone in the entire event map, Use Maple Points to get Fantasy Coins.
➢ Lucky Draw Candy!: 3 times a day to get random enhancement items.

Currency used for Night Troupe: Fantasy Coin

    Spiegelmann's Time Travel Ticket


    One Way Ticket

    21st August 2024, (after v235 Patch) ~ 17th September 2024, 2359hrs

    Available to Level 101+, Zero character who has completed Chapter 2 for Story Quest
    Look for the [Night Troupe] Receive a Time Travel Ticket quest in the Icon to begin.


  • There are 2 types of Time Travel Ticket you can choose from.
  • You can only choose 1 Time Travel Ticket per week.
  • After choosing a Time Travel Ticket, eliminate level range monsters will give 12 Fantasy Points.
  • 1 Fantasy Coin = 100 Fantasy Points.
  • Time Travel Ticket for the week ends at every Wednesday at 1000hrs.
  • You can only reselect the Time Travel Ticket after every Wednesday at 2359hrs.
  • Progression is shared within the Same World.
  • Once you reach the activation limit, you can no longer activate the skill until it resets midnight.
  • Eos Hourglass and Helios Hourglass activations is shared by all characters in the same world.

    Eos Hourglass

  • Defeat 500 level range monsters to activate an EXP Buff for 150 seconds.
  • You can only activate this Hourglass skill 5 times.
  • Depending on the Designer Grade, you will receive more EXP.
  • Junior: 350% / Senior: 400% / Veteran: 450% / Master: 500%

    Helios Hourglass

  • Defeat 500 level range monsters to activate an EXP Buff for 150 seconds.
  • You can only activate this Hourglass skill 25 times.
  • Depending on the Designer Grade, you will receive more EXP.
  • Junior: 200% / Senior: 250% / Veteran: 300% / Master: 350%

    Sneak Attack! Block Papulatus


    Gott'em!

    28th August 2024, 1000hrs ~ 17th September 2024, 2359hrs



    Available to Level 200+, Zero character who has completed Chapter 2 for Story Quest
    Requirement: Senior and above
    Look for the [Night Troupe] Sneak Attack! Block Papulatus quest from NPC Time Rift to begin.

  • Fantasy Coins and EXP will be given upon completion depending on the points obtain.
      ➢ 1~49 points = 100 Fantasy Coins

      ➢ 750~1149 points = 1000 Fantasy Coins

  • Additional Notice
    •  ↪ Rewards can only be obtained once per character per week.
     ↪ Rewards can be obtain again after every Wednesday at 11:59pm.


    Exciting Doll Draw


    The EXP continues

    28th August 2024, 1000hrs ~ 17th September 2024, 2359hrs



    Available to Level 101+, Zero character who has completed Chapter 2 for Story Quest
    Requirement: Senior and above who reached the daily 300 Fantasy Coin cap.
    Look for the [Night Troupe] Exciting Doll Draw! quest in from NPC Roly-Poly 1 Doll to begin.

  • Click Start and click the arrow button left or right.
  • Once ready, click the Yellow Star Button to grab a doll.
  • 1st Successful Draw : 100 EXP Coupon
  • 2nd Successful Draw : 200 EXP Coupon
  • 3rd Successful Draw : 300 EXP Coupon
  • Fail Draw : 50 EXP Coupon
  • If it is a fail draw, you can no longer play for the day.
  • If it is a successful draw, you can continue to grab a doll up to 3 draws.





    Beep, Beep Bumper Cars


    Bump

    4th September 2024, 1000hrs ~ 17th September 2024, 2359hrs



    Available to Level 101+, Zero character who has completed Chapter 2 for Story Quest
    Requirement: Veteran and above.
    Look for the [Night Troupe] Go! Beep, Beep Bumper Cars Info quest in the Star Icon or NPC Sepi to begin.

    Controls : Arrow Keys (Movement) , Spacebar (Booster)

  • Queue for a 3v3 Bumper Car match and get Levels!
  • Just simply push the opponent out of the map and win.
      ➢ All participants : Random levels
      ➢ Winners : Guaranteed 1 Level + Random levels
      ➢ Losers : Random levels
  • Levels are given to all participants regardless Win or Lose.
  • Levels given are similar to Extreme Growth Potion (141-199).
  • Characters level 220 or higher will receive same EXP equivalent to level 219.
  • Entry Limit: 3 times a week, reset every Wednesday
  • Entry is only available during 10:00am to 1:59am.
