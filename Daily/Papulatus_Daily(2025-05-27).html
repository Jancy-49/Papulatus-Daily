Regarding System Updates in v242 NEXT Update


27 May 2025

Regarding System Updates in v242 NEXT Update



Dear Maplers,

Starting from the NEXT Update happening on Wednesday, 4 June 2025, the following contents will be modified.

Cross World Maple Auction


To support the migration to a cross-world auction system, the current Auction House system will be undergoing some transitions, therefore if you have any items in the Auction House, they can be claimed by pressing the "Receive Existing Auction Items" button in the Completed tab of the Maple Auction UI by June 7, 2026, 11:59 pm.
The "Receive Existing Auction Items" button will be removed after June 8, 2026, at 12:00 am, and the items left unclaimed and expired will be automatically deleted.
This will also be applied to the items that have no restrictions on the claim period (i.e. were traded before November 24, 2017). These items can also be claimed using this button, and the items will no longer be accessible after June 8, 2026, at 12:00 am.

Cross World Meso Market


As the Meso Markets are being unified, please note that if there are any transactions registered in the Meso Market before the patch on June 4, 2025 (Wednesday), the Mesos will be deposited into the storage of the world that used the Meso Market, and Maple Points will be credited to the account.

Dimension Library - Maple Chronicle


The â€˜Book of Arcane Riverâ€™ will be temporarily inaccessible after maintenance on June 4, 2025 (Wednesday). The book will reopen with a future update.

Hyper Teleport Rock


After the maintenance on June 4, 2025 (Wednesday), all characters possessing a permanent Hyper Teleport Rock will automatically gain permanent access to the new Premium World Map Teleport feature which allows you to teleport freely via the World Map UI with no cooldown.
If your permanent Hyper Teleport Rock is stored in Cash Storage, it will be replaced with a Premium World Map Teleport Pass (Permanent). Using this ticket will unlock the permanent Premium World Map Teleport feature for your character.
If your character owns multiple Permanent Hyper Teleport Rocks, you will receive additional Premium World Map Teleport Pass(es) (Permanent) as items. For example:

  • 1 Permanent Hyper Teleport Rock = Auto-unlock via World Map UI
  • 2 Permanent Hyper Teleport Rocks = 1 auto-unlock + 1 pass as an item
  • 3 Permanent Hyper Teleport Rocks = 1 auto-unlock + 2 passes
  • ... and so forth
