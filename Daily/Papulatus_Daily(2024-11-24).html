[Updated] Unscheduled Channel Maintenance - November 23, 2024


[Updated 12:45 PM PST] The unscheduled channel maintenance has been completed. Thank you for your patience.

Saturday, November 23, 2024

CET (UTC +1): 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM 
AEDT (UTC +11): 3:00 AM (November 24) - 5:00 AM (November 24)

    • Group A

      • Channel 1-5 on EU Burning
      • Cross World Party Quest

    • Resolved an issue where Zero characters on Heroic servers would sometimes not receive loot from Chaos Zakum and Akechi Mitsuhide.

Unscheduled Emergency Maintenance- November 23, 2024


[Updated 8:50 PM PST] The maintenance has been completed and we have extended Cash Shop items in your inventory for 3 hours. Please restart your Nexon Launcher or Steam to make sure that you get the latest client! Thank you for your patience.

[Updated 8:36 PM PST] We anticipate the maintenance to conclude around 9:00 PM PST (12:00 AM EST November 24 / 6:00 AM CET November 24 / 4:00 PM AEDT November 24).

Hi Maplers,

We are aware of an issue with boss rewards and their drop rates. To resolve the issues for affected players, we'll be temporarily shutting the game down. The game servers will be inaccessible until further notice. We will make sure this is resolved as quickly as possible and will provide updates when we can.

Thank you for your patience and we apologize for any inconvenience this issue may have caused. We will be shutting the game down today, November 23 2024 at 6:00 PM PST (9:00 PM EST / 3:00 AM CET November 24 / 1:00 PM AEDT November 24).

Thank you for your cooperation,

The MapleStory Team

  • Changes and Updates:
    • Resolved an issue where the drop rate for boss rewards was incorrect after the v255 update.
